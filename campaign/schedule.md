## Posting Rules

- No em dashes (people see them as AI-generated)
- Keep under 1,300 characters for LinkedIn algorithm (or use "see more" break strategically)
- First line must hook -- question, bold claim, or surprising statement
- End with hashtags (5 max, mix of broad and niche)
- Post between 8-10am ET for best reach
- Engage with every comment within 2 hours

## Campaign Arc

```
Post 1: "I build real AI tools" (proof)
Post 2: "I do this for fun too" (passion + depth)
Post 3: "I have a system for it" (methodology)
Post 4: "I applied it to my own life" (relatable + insight)
Post 5: "Here's what your company needs" (thought leadership + CTA)
```

Each post builds on the last. By Post 5, anyone reading the series sees:
- Technical depth (MCP server, NLP, model training)
- Engineering leadership (teams, compliance, architecture)
- AI-native thinking (methodology, not just tools)
- Self-awareness and authenticity (job search, hobbies, honest about what works)

## Amplification

- Share each post to relevant LinkedIn groups (AI, HealthTech, Engineering Leadership)
- Tag relevant people in comments (not the post itself) when natural
- Cross-reference posts: "In my last post I showed X, this week I'm showing Y"
- Pin Post 5 to profile when it goes live (it's the closest to a CTA)

## Metrics to Track

- Impressions per post
- Profile views per week (baseline now, measure after Post 5)
- Inbound messages / connection requests from hiring managers or recruiters
- Any direct mentions from target companies (Florida Blue, Cotiviti, etc.)

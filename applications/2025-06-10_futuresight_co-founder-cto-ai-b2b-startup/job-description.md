Description

FutureSight is hiring an experienced and visionary Founding CTO for a new B2B SaaS company reimagining Customer Success through AI. This venture is building the next stage of Customer Success Management by proactively engaging every account to retain, expand, and delight. The platform monitors usage signals, surfaces next-best actions, and generates hyper-personalized outreach—unlocking higher revenue retention, net-new expansion, and leadership-ready insights for customer teams.

We’re aiming to launch in Q3 2025.

The idea is validated, early adopters are lining up, and the studio team is ready to build with you.

As Founding CTO, You Will:
Own the technical vision — Define architecture, AI approach, and long-term platform strategy
Build the product — Partner with FutureSight’s product/engineering team to ship V1
Run Pilots — Land lighthouse customers, iterate pricing/positioning, andvalidate retention + expansion
Scale the tech stack — Design for scale, extensibility, and CS security best practices
Raise capital — Support the seed round with a strong product narrative and traction
Build your team — Hire and lead the engineering org as the company grows

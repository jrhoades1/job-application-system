Job Description

About the Role

OpenLoop is looking for a strategic and hands-on Director of Engineering to join our team remotely in the San Francisco Bay area. In this role, you will be responsible for driving the development of our technology roadmap, lead high-performing engineering teams, and integrate AI-powered solutions into our digital health platform.

What You'll Do

Provide strategic leadership and technical oversight across engineering functions (platform, infrastructure, product development, AI/ML, DevOps).

Drive the development and implementation of scalable, secure, and compliant healthcare software solutions.

Partner cross-functionally with Product, Design, Clinical, and Data teams to define and execute the technical vision.

Build and scale AI-driven features that enhance clinical workflows, patient engagement, and operational efficiency.

Foster a culture of innovation, ownership, and continuous improvement.

Lead hiring, mentoring, and development of engineering talent, promoting diversity and inclusion.

Ensure adherence to security, privacy, and healthcare compliance standards (HIPAA, SOC 2, etc.).

Manage vendor relationships and evaluate build vs. buy decisions for critical technologies.

Other duties as assigned

Who You Are

Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.

8-10+ years of software engineering experience, with 5+ in engineering leadership roles.

Proven success leading and scaling engineering teams in high-growth startups or tech-forward healthcare environments.

Hands-on experience implementing AI/ML solutions, especially in healthcare or enterprise software.

Deep understanding of cloud-based architecture (e.g., AWS, GCP), modern frameworks, and API design.

Experience with healthcare interoperability standards (e.g., HL7, FHIR) is a plus.

Strong communication skills with the ability to translate technical strategy into business outcomes.

Passionate about improving healthcare delivery through technology.

Remote Salary Range

$220,000—$255,000 USD

 

About OpenLoop

OpenLoop was co-founded by CEO, Dr. Jon Lensing, and COO, Christian Williams, with the vision to bring healing anywhere. Our tele-health support solutions are thoughtfully designed to streamline and simplify go-to-market care delivery for companies offering meaningful virtual support to patients across an expansive array of specialties, in all 50 states.

Our Company Culture

We have a relatively flat organizational structure here at OpenLoop. Everyone is encouraged to bring ideas to the table and make things happen. This fits in well with our core values of Autonomy, Competence and Belonging, as we want everyone to feel empowered and supported to do their best work.

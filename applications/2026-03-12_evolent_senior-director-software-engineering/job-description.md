Protect your privacy:
Recruitment fraud notice
Beware of fraudulent individuals posing as Evolent Human Resources/Talent Acquisition representatives through fake e-mail addresses and text messaging systems.
Don't Download
We will never ask you to download an external app. We will always communicate with you directly through emails with an @evolent.com domain or via our Workday tracking system.
Verified Employees
If you receive LinkedIn outreach from someone identifying themselves as an Evolent employee, make sure that their profile has a checkmark showing that their workplace was verified using their work address.
Protect Privacy
Our recruiters will never ask for sensitive or other compromising information such as bank account details or ask you to download money wiring apps such as Zelle or Apple Pay.
If you are unsure of the legitimacy of a recruitment outreach for a position with our company, please reach out to
recruitingteam@evolent.com
and someone from our team will follow up with you.
If you believe you are a victim of recruitment fraud, please contact your local law enforcement authorities or file a complaint with the
Federal Trade Commission
.

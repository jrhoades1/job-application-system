Job Description
About the job

Our client’s mission is to transform healthcare by embedding AI directly into medical workflows and automating the administrative tasks that slow down patient care. Their technology enhances efficiency, reduces errors, and enables real-time clinical decision-making at scale.

This is your chance to shape the future of agentic AI in healthcare. As Head of Engineering , you’ll take a hands-on leadership role building systems that clinicians and patients can trust. You’ll scale both the engineering team and the product, working directly with the founding team to deliver AI agents that change how care is delivered.

About the Role

The Head of Engineering will be responsible for architecting, designing, and implementing next-generation agentic AI systems. This is both a leadership and hands-on technical role, with direct accountability for scaling engineering around agent-based architectures. You’ll set the technical direction, mentor engineers, and build core product capabilities from scratch while elevating existing systems.

What You’ll Be Working On

Architect, design, and implement scalable agentic AI systems
Scale engineering teams dedicated to AI agent development
Collaborate closely with co-founders to define technical strategy and product roadmap
Build and enhance core components of the product, including from scratch when needed
Drive applied GenAI, LLMOps, and LLM evaluation capabilities into production systems
Deliver systems that are used by real clinicians and healthcare organizations at scale

What You’ll Bring

Proven leadership in fast-paced startup environments, with strong people management skills
Hands-on expertise in agentic AI frameworks (at least one deeply, familiarity with others)
Experience architecting and deploying scalable AI/LLM-based systems
Strong knowledge of LLMs (training, fine-tuning, integration into production-grade software)
Deep experience with React plus either Python or Node.js
Familiarity with cloud (GCP, Azure, AWS) and DevOps (CI/CD pipelines)
Experience with both SQL and NoSQL databases
Knowledge of applied GenAI, LLMOps, and evaluation practices

Tech Stack

Frontend: ReactJS, Typescript
Backend: Node.js or Python
Databases: SQL + NoSQL
DevOps: CI/CD, GCP, Azure, AWS
AI: Applied GenAI, LLMOps, LLM evaluations

Why This Role

This isn’t just another engineering leadership position. It’s a rare opportunity to define and scale agentic AI for healthcare at a venture-backed company already transforming the patient care cycle. You’ll have the autonomy to build, the mandate to scale, and the chance to lead technology that will directly improve healthcare outcomes.

🔒 This role is being managed exclusively by NuPeople on behalf of our client. All inquiries will remain strictly confidential.

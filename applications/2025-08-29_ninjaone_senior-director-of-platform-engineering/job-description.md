About the Role
As our Senior Director of Platform Engineering, you’ll be instrumental in defining and managing the NinjaOne platform. In this high-impact leadership role, you'll shape the evolution of the NinjaOne Platform—guiding how we build, scale, and deliver our products to customers around the world. We're looking for a strategic, execution-focused leader with deep experience delivering software at scale, a passion for exceptional customer experiences, and a strong track record of platform innovation.

Join our fast-growing engineering team as we expand beyond our leading endpoint management solution for MSPs into broader Enterprise markets. Your vision and leadership will directly influence the core of how NinjaOne powers modern IT operations.

Location - We are flexible on remote working from home, if you are located in the USA and reside in one of the following states - CA, CO, CT, FL, GA, *IL, KS, ME, MA, NJ, NC, NY, OR, TN, TX, VA, and WA. We have physical offices in Austin, TX and Tampa, FL, if you prefer a hybrid option.

We hire the best software engineers, but experience in our stack can’t hurt:  NinjaOne is built on Java, Kotlin, C++, Golang and Postgres, supporting millions of user endpoints and running as a scalable cloud service in AWS.  Knowing large-scale datastore bottlenecks, asynchronous application design and client-server architecture will help you.

What You’ll be Doing
Be our subject matter expert for all things Cloud and Platform related at NinjaOne
Collaborate with our Development, Product Management, Sales, Marketing, IT, Compliance, and Security teams to manage, develop and grow existing Platform to deliver NinjaOne products globally 
Contribute to the strategy on market positioning, identify appropriate architectural concepts needed to expand globally and improve resiliency of the NinjaOne product portfolio 
Own product requirements to ensure features needed to scale NinjaOne products globally are prioritized with Product Management 
Ensure customer confidence in NinjaOne by designing and building robust services to meet NinjaOne Service Level Agreements 
Manage and grow Platform team to support the needs of NinjaOne’s products
Ensure the governance and commitment to compliance frameworks such as ISO 270001, SOX, SOC-2, NIST 800-53 rev 5 and more.
Identify bottlenecks and drive efficiency in our SDLC process
Help manage and mitigate runaway cost in our Cloud Infrastructure
Other duties as needed

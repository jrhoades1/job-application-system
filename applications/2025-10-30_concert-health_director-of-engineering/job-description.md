Job Description
About the job

Who We Are

Concert Health is the leading national behavioral health medical group committed to integrating the Collaborative Care model into existing health care practices to improve patients’ lives. We believe everyone, regardless of location or insurance, should have easy access to personalized, high-quality behavioral health support that is guided by the provider they trust.

Our Collaborative Care program effectively targets and treats most behavioral health conditions, while embracing behavioral health as central to the patient's care journey and overall health. With its clinical excellence and operational efficiency, Concert Health is the preferred partner for health systems and medical groups seeking to deliver integrated behavioral health care that is both scalable and sustainable.

We’re looking for an extensive hands-on engineering leader to serve in a critical senior leadership role, helping scale clinical operations to meet the needs of millions of patients.

Who You Are

We’re looking for a seasoned, entrepreneurial, hands-on technology leader to assume leadership of our existing engineering team and partner with our Chief Product & Technology Officer to execute our vision for an end-to-end technology platform that empowers our clinical team to provide excellent care at scale. You are excited to be architecting and building a modern platform for an industry changing tech-enabled health care services.

You enjoy leading teams focused on building, scaling and operating efficient engineering programs. You are excited to take ownership of our software engineering & architecture. You have deep architecture and technical chops and can guide the team in building a scalable and robust platform, and roll up your sleeves to code as needed. You love the challenge of making sure roadmap targets are achieved with solutions that are scalable, balanced, and architecturally sound and secure, and ensuring that technical debt is incurred consciously and methodically repaid. You enjoy mentoring developers, supporting their career development and thrive in a fast paced, dynamic, remote, async culture.

You have deep expertise across frontend (React) and backend (Typescript, Python, C#) technologies, data architecture and governance, and experience in building modern software platforms on AWS, including integrating with platforms such as Salesforce and other enterprise systems. Preferably, you have experience with EHR integrations using HL7/FHIR, building data-informed products, and integrating AI technologies such as LLMs to enhance functionality and user experience.

Responsibilities:

Lead, mentor, and inspire a small multidisciplinary engineering team while delivering high-quality software that empowers clinicians and enhances the patient experience.
Partner closely with the CPTO and product team to define the technology strategy, including data and AI strategies to make informed build vs. buy vs. integrate decisions, aligning the roadmap with technical debt and architecture priorities.
Architect and build systems on AWS, and custom Salesforce applications that are secure, scalable, resilient, and maintainable, leveraging modern cloud technologies.
Lead the EHR integration strategy, overseeing integrations with multiple health systems and ensuring high-fidelity, reliable data exchange using standards such as HL7 and FHIR.
Build and maintain robust data pipelines to support analytics, and reporting.
Drive the integration of data-informed features and AI technologies, including LLMs, to enhance product functionality and user experience.
Promote standardization of system design, enforce software development best practices, implement and maintain reliable CI/CD processes, and ensure clear documentation of systems, processes, and technical decisions.
Build and sustain a high-performing engineering culture focused on excellence, reliability, productivity, continuous learning, and growth.
Manage technology investments, vendors, and budget.

Qualifications:

B.S. or MS. Computer Science, Engineering or relevant experience
10+ years of experience in software engineering, including 5+ years in technical leadership or architecture roles (e.g., Engineering Manager, Director).
Expertise in designing, building, and scaling high-quality, resilient, and observable software on AWS, including distributed backend systems (Typescript, Python, C#, Java) and production-grade frontend applications (React)
Proven success leading multidisciplinary engineering teams in fast-paced, product-driven environments, including remote and asynchronous teams.
Proven ability to implement scalable CI/CD processes that support fast iteration while maintaining system reliability and security.
Exceptional communication and collaboration skills across technical and non-technical stakeholders.
Deep understanding of agile development and lean product methodologies, with experience balancing roadmap delivery and technical debt.

Preferred Experience:

Background in healthcare technology, clinical systems integration, or compliance-driven environments (e.g., HIPAA, SOC 2).
Experience working with electronic health records (EHRs) and healthcare interoperability standards such as HL7 and FHIR.
Prior experience in early-stage or growth-stage startup environments, scaling both engineering teams and technology platforms.
Experience building data-informed products and integrating AI/ML technologies into user-facing applications.
Experience building Salesforce applications, including integrations with other systems and leveraging platform capabilities through custom code (Apex, Lightning, or similar).

About the job

About Hopscotch Primary Care

At Hopscotch Primary Care, we believe great healthcare should be accessible to all people across all communities. Today, almost 20% of Americans live in a rural community, yet only 11% of physicians practice in those same communities. We are on a mission to transform healthcare in rural America. We provide high-quality primary care tailored to meet the needs of our patients through our robust care model and comprehensive care team, delivering care in our clinics, and across settings, and wrapping resources around the patients who need them most.

Our patients and the care teams who serve them sit at the center of everything we do at Hopscotch. Hopscotch Primary Care takes a team approach to serve patient needs and provide the best care possible. Our goal is to provide the care each of us would want for ourselves or for our family members, in the right setting, and at the right time.

Today, we are serving thousands of patients in our value-based care model and the number is growing every day. If you want to bring your experience, skill and passion to make a lasting impact in healthcare, we'd like to meet you.

About The Role

Hopscotch Primary Care is looking for a hands-on Senior Director of Engineering to lead our Platform and Data Engineering teams at a pivotal moment in our growth. The ideal candidate will blend strategic vision with technical execution, overseeing developers, increasing development velocity without compromising quality, and building out our internal engineering capabilities. Our data-driven care model relies on a strong technical foundation to power our clinical operations, analytics, and patient engagement. This leader will shape the architecture and execution of scalable infrastructure and data systems, ensuring they scale as we grow across new markets. In our fast-paced, dynamic environment, you will work closely with our Product, Operations, Growth and Clinical teams to define and drive our technology roadmap.

Responsibilities

Strategic Leadership

Develop a deep understanding of the current architecture, product strategy and company vision to set a comprehensive technical strategy
Partner with Product, Clinical and Operations to assess vendors in build / buy decisions balancing the strategic needs and available resources within the company
Effectively communicate the technology vision with all levels and stakeholders across Hopscotch

Team Management & Development

Lead, mentor, and grow a team of high-performing platform engineers and data engineers
Foster a culture of technical excellence, accountability, and continuous learning

Technology & Architecture:

Oversee the design and implementation of scalable, secure, and high-performing infrastructure and data pipelines
Ensure platform reliability, observability, and performance as we scale
Drive the adoption of best practices in cloud-native architecture, CI/CD, data governance, and DevOps

Cross-functional Collaboration:

Partner with Product, Growth, Operations, and Analytics to deliver tools and platforms that support our care model including build or integration with vended solutions
Enable faster experimentation and decision-making through self-serve data and infrastructure tools
Demonstrate ability to be customer facing when appropriate (clinicians, patients)

Delivery & Execution:

Translate company objectives into engineering roadmaps with clear milestones, resourcing, and metrics
Guide technical delivery while balancing speed, quality, and sustainability

Experience leading a hybrid model of in-house development and vended solutions
A track record of developing high-performing technical teams
Experience in a start-up or high-growth environment, ideally in healthcare
A results-driven mindset, focusing time and resources against the most impactful priorities
A concise and articulate communication style, breaking complex concepts down both verbally and in writing

From a cultural perspective, you are:

Customer-focused with a passion for serving patients and providers who make healthcare possible
Agile, yet thoughtful, and can work effectively in a fast-paced, ever-changing environment
Constantly seeking ways to simplify and improve current processes, bringing a positive attitude to challenges
Accountable, holding yourself and others to a high standard and seeking to support and develop others

ADDITIONAL QUALIFICATIONS:

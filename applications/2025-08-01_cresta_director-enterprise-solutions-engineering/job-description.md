About the role:

As a Director, Enterprise Solutions Engineering at Cresta, you will lead a team of high-performing pre-sales Solutions Engineers supporting complex sales cycles within our Enterprise segment. You’ll partner closely with Enterprise sales leadership to align SE coverage with pipeline priorities, coach your team through technical discovery and solution design, and ensure executional excellence across demos, POCs, and technical validation.

In this player-coach role, you’ll also support key strategic deals directly—providing hands-on technical leadership, influencing the solution design, and helping shape the business case alongside Enterprise Account Executives. Internally, you will collaborate cross-functionally with Product, Engineering, and Enablement to elevate the SE team’s impact and drive continuous improvement in how we position and deliver Cresta’s platform.

Location Preference : East or Central, US Remote

Responsibilities:  

Conduct weekly 1:1s and team meetings to coach SE's on deal strategy, technical execution, and best practices
Create and maintain individualized development plans; lead monthly ride-alongs and collaborate with SE Enablement on targeted training
Align closely with Enterprise Sales Leaders through weekly syncs, sales leadership meetings, and coverage reviews to drive segment strategy
Run weekly SE pipeline reviews to ensure technical qualification, surface systemic blockers, and share performance metrics with leadership
Manage SE team capacity and utilization, coordinate cross-regional support, and drive recruiting and onboarding with structured 30-60-90 day plans
Provide hands-on support for 2–3 strategic enterprise accounts by leading technical discovery and shaping technical strategy for deals >$500K
Collaborate with Product to influence the roadmap, lead Enterprise-specific POC initiatives, and validate complex implementations
Develop and deliver high-impact demos and presentations; co-design POCs that accelerate sales cycles for priority enterprise opportunities
Establish and scale technical best practices, discovery frameworks, and competitive enablement assets across the Enterprise SE team

Qualifications We Value:

8+ years of experience in sales engineering or technical sales, with 2+ years in a leadership capacity
Deep expertise in Contact Center technologies, including Conversational AI, CCaaS platforms, and telephony infrastructure
Understanding of AI Agent applications, with hands-on experience integrating AI into customer workflows
Strong working knowledge of enterprise platforms including Salesforce, Google, Amazon, contact center infrastructure, and enterprise SaaS environments
Known for resourcefulness, strategic thinking, and a collaborative mindset when working cross-functionally to drive deal success
Passionate about Sales Engineering career development, with a focus on coaching SEs through structured growth plans, role clarity, and skills development
Experience in building mentoring frameworks that foster technical excellence, soft skill growth, and leadership readiness within the SE organization
Embody and promote our core Operating Principles through mentorship, leadership, and hands-on engagement

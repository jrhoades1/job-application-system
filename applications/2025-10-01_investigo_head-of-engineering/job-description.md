About the job Head of Engineering (Remote – US)

AI Infrastructure | Early-Stage Startup

We’re working with a fast-growing AI infrastructure startup that’s building the tooling and systems to support next-generation model development — from distributed training platforms to inference optimization and data pipelines. Own the engineering strategy and execution across core infrastructure, platform services, and DevOps Partner with the CEO and CTO to shape product and architectural vision Lead hiring, mentorship, and performance management for a high-caliber, remote-first team Oversee the development of performant, scalable systems for ML model training, deployment, and observability Drive best practices around CI/CD, reliability, and developer productivity Collaborate cross-functionally with research, product, and GTM teams in a fast-paced, experimental environment

🧠 Tech Environment:

Golang, Rust, Python Kubernetes, Terraform, Argo, Airflow Vector DBs, object stores, and distributed file systems GPU orchestration, LLM fine-tuning pipelines, autoscaling APIs Major cloud (AWS/GCP), on-prem/hybrid clusters

✅ You Might Be a Fit If:

8+ years in software engineering, including 3+ in leadership roles Experience scaling infra teams and systems at high-growth startups Deep knowledge of distributed systems, cloud-native architectures, and developer tooling Bonus: Familiarity with ML/AI infra tooling (e.g. Ray, Triton, Hugging Face, Weights & Biases, etc.) You thrive in ambiguous, 0→1 environments and are excited by technical moonshots

Job Description

About the job Vice President, Product & Engineering

We’re seeking a VP of Product & Engineering to lead strategy, execution, and team building for a category-defining platform at the intersection of distributed systems, networking, and AI-driven security. This role blends visionary product leadership with deep technical oversight, driving the roadmap from concept to scale.

You’ll partner with the CEO to shape market fit, guide architecture, and grow a world-class team—while being hands-on across design, execution, and innovation.

Responsibilities

Define and execute product and engineering strategy across core platform components. Translate customer and market insights into requirements and technical execution. Oversee architecture to deliver secure, scalable, and high-performance solutions. Drive full lifecycle development—from prototype to release—with quality and speed. Build, mentor, and scale a high-performing product and engineering organization. Lead innovation and contribute to our IP portfolio in networking and security.

Qualifications

10+ years leading product and/or engineering in high-growth environments. Strong technical background in cybersecurity, networking, or distributed systems. Proven ability to take products from vision to market adoption. Startup DNA: hands-on, fast-moving, and experienced in scaling teams. Excellent communicator with customers, investors, and cross-functional partners.

What We Offer

Early equity and leadership ownership in a high-growth company. Ground-floor opportunity to redefine Internet trust and disrupt a $60B+ market. Work at the forefront of cryptography, trust-based routing, and AI-powered security.

Featured benefits

Medical insurance, Vision insurance, Dental insurance, 401(k), Paid paternity leave, Paid maternity leave

About the Opportunity

We are conducting a confidential search for a Head of Engineering to lead a high-impact engineering team at a venture-backed technology company modernizing one of the most critical yet outdated industries - insurance.

Our mission is to transform claims processing from a slow, manual, and error-prone operation into a seamless, intelligent, and data-driven experience. From self-service claim filing to fraud detection powered by machine learning, our platform powers claims workflows for some of the world’s largest insurers.

This is an opportunity to lead an exceptional engineering team that’s tackling complex, meaningful problems at scale - designing systems that directly impact how billions of dollars move through the insurance ecosystem every year.

What You’ll Do

Leadership & Execution

Lead, mentor, and grow a talented team of software engineers focused on developing and scaling core product capabilities.
Drive technical direction and execution across multiple projects, ensuring alignment with product and business goals.
Balance strategic leadership with hands-on technical contribution, especially in shaping architectural decisions and reviewing critical code paths.

Team Growth & Development

Conduct regular 1:1s and performance reviews, fostering professional growth and technical excellence.
Build a culture of accountability, empathy, and innovation, where engineers are empowered to own outcomes and learn continuously.
Champion recruiting, onboarding, and retention efforts to scale a world-class engineering organization.

Process & Quality

Implement scalable engineering processes that enable velocity while maintaining reliability, performance, and security.
Collaborate cross-functionally with Product, Design, and Data Science to ensure quality, predictability, and customer value.
Establish clear goals, KPIs, and feedback loops to measure success and continuously improve delivery.

Strategic Impact

Partner with senior leadership to shape the technical roadmap and organizational strategy as the company continues to grow rapidly.
Anticipate scaling challenges - in people, systems, and processes - and design frameworks to manage them effectively.
Serve as a voice of engineering excellence and innovation, advocating for modern development practices and continuous improvement.

What You Bring

10+ years of software engineering experience, with at least 7+ years leading or managing teams in high-growth or startup environments.
Proven ability to deliver high-quality software at scale , balancing innovation, stability, and business needs.
Experience managing full lifecycle product development - from architecture and planning through deployment and maintenance.
Strong leadership presence, with the ability to influence across teams, mentor engineers, and foster a psychologically safe environment.
Deep understanding of modern engineering practices: CI/CD, cloud infrastructure, performance optimization, and secure development.
Technology-agnostic mindset - capable of leading diverse technical stacks and learning new tools quickly.
Current environment includes: Node.js (TypeScript), React, GraphQL, Python, PostgreSQL, Docker, AWS.

In Your First 90 Days, You’ll

Gain deep context on our mission, culture, and platform architecture.
Establish strong relationships across engineering, product, and leadership teams.
Define clear goals and development plans for your team.
Identify process improvements that streamline delivery and elevate code quality.
Contribute to product strategy and long-term technical vision.

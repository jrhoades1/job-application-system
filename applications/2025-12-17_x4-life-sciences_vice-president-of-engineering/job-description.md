About the job VP of Engineering - Telehealth

About the Opportunity

X4 Life Sciences is proud to be partnered with growth stage telehealth in the neurology care delivery space. The company partners with hospitals and health systems across the country to deliver timely, high-quality stroke and neurology care.

We are seeking an experienced Vice President of Engineering to lead the product engineering organization and deliver best-in-class digital experiences for hospital users, physicians, and clinical operations teams. This role will oversee engineering teams building and scaling solutions such as hospital-facing apps, mobile applications, physician care coordination tools, and internal clinical operations systems.

Key Responsibilities

Define and execute the engineering vision, roadmap, and technical strategy aligned with product and company goals. Lead full product lifecycle delivery across web, mobile, and internal tools, including video consultation and secure SSO platforms. Build, mentor, and scale a high-performing, distributed engineering team (U.S. & offshore). Promote modern engineering practices: DevOps, CI/CD, infrastructure-as-code, and AI-driven development tools. Collaborate with Product, Data & AI, and Clinical teams to deliver scalable, secure, and reliable solutions. Serve as a technical leader and represent engineering across the organization and externally.

Qualifications & Skills

10+ years in software engineering with 5+ years in senior leadership roles (VP/Director). Proven experience delivering scalable SaaS applications (web & mobile) in healthcare. Hands-on experience with video communication platforms and federated SSO. Strong knowledge of cloud infrastructure, DevOps, CI/CD, and AI/ML development tools. Experienced in building and scaling distributed engineering teams. Strategic thinker with excellent communication skills and executive presence.

If you are a highly qualified and experience software leader in the telehealth space, and this look like a good fit for you. Please reach out to o.archer@x4lifesciences.com.

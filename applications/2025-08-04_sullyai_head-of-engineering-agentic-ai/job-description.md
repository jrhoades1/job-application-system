Job Description
About the job

About Us

Sully.ai is transforming healthcare access by integrating AI into medical workflows and automating healthcare administrative tasks throughout the patient visit cycle - enhancing efficiency, reducing errors, and supporting real-time decision-making.

What You'll Do

We are seeking an exceptional Head of Engineering with hands-on experience in architecting, designing, and implementing agentic AI systems to play a pivotal hands-on role in shaping the current and future capabilities of our product.
Scale our agents and engineering team focused around each agent
Work closely with the co-founders and be responsible for building core components and elevating our product to the next level.
Enhance existing systems and build new ones from scratch, either independently or by leading other engineers.

What You'll Bring

Strong people leadership experience and bias for action
Must have hands-on experience in architecting, designing and implementing agentic AI systems
Very strong experience in architecting and implementing scalable agentic systems
Hands-on experience using at least one agentic framework and familiarity with other agentic frameworks
Hands-on experience using cutting edge LLM, fine-tuning LLM and integrating them in production grade software used by real users
Proven track record in fast-paced startup environments and collaborating with global teams
Must have expertise in React and Python or Node.js
Knowledge of ML, AI, LLMs

Tech Stack

Stack: ReactJS, Typescript, Node.js

Databases: NoSQL and SQL

DevOps: CI/CD pipelines, cloud services (GCP, Azure, AWS)

AI: Experience with Applied GenAI, LLMOps, and LLM evaluations

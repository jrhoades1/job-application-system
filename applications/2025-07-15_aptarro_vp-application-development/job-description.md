Job Description

VP Application Development Full Time Atlanta, Georgia +4 More Locations 18 days ago Requisition ID: 1075

Apply Salary Range: $180,000.00 To $200,000.00 Annually As Vice President of Application Development, you will lead Aptarro's product engineering teams responsible for ClaimStaker and RCE, our mission-critical platforms. This net-new leadership role is responsible for timely, high-quality delivery across application engineering, with a mandate to modernize our architecture, elevate execution, and align closely with our go-to-market and product strategy. This is a hands-on, high-impact leadership role. You will manage engineering teams for two .NET-based applications, and partner with peers in Product, QA, DevOps, and Cloud Engineering to ensure our platforms deliver value at scale to enterprise and mid-market customers.

You will be instrumental in:

Extending the product capabilities to meet emerging needs for the RCE and SaaS RCE customers. Leading modernization of our ClaimStaker platform. Strengthening engineering execution and operational rigor to support consistent and timely releases. Advancing reusable components across ClaimStaker and RCE to drive efficiency and innovation. Contributing to internal platform development (e.g., content authoring tools) to improve velocity and quality.

Success in this role will be measured by:

Timely delivery of platform enhancements and new features aligned with product roadmap. Progress on cloud migration milestones for RCE and ClaimStaker. Improved SDLC maturity, release predictability, and operational escalation processes. Engineering contributions to AI, internal tooling, and convergence efforts.

Key Responsibilities

Leadership & Strategy

Operate as a hands-on leader ("player/coach") of the application development organization. Lead teams responsible for ClaimStaker and RCE, with an emphasis on architectural modernization, delivery consistency, and cross-platform synergy. Partner with Product and Customer Operations to translate roadmap priorities into executable plans. Own convergence strategy between ClaimStaker and RCE, including reusable rules engines and content alignment.

Engineering Execution

Oversee upgrades and modernization efforts, including Angular 17 upgrade and refactoring for cloud-readiness. Guide the evolution of Cloud Rules and internal content tools to increase supportability and user value. Ensure execution of release processes for ClaimStaker and RCE aligned with SDLC. Institutionalize operations-to-engineering escalation processes for rapid issue response.

Platform Modernization & Convergence

Drive progress on the cloud migration of ClaimStaker in alignment with Cloud Engineering. Evaluate pathways to componentize ClaimStaker/CodeWizard rules for use within RCE. Lead tradeoff analysis and decision-making on architectural convergence between platforms.

Innovation & Internal Tools

Partner on the development and MVP delivery of internal content authoring tools. Support initiatives related to AI/CAC pilots and content automation strategies. Contribute to the creation of scalable engineering processes and reusable infrastructure.

Culture & Talent Development

Build and lead high-performing, engaged engineering teams with clear accountability and direction. Foster a culture of quality, ownership, and cross-functional collaboration. Mentor technical leaders and engineers to grow expertise in cloud-native development, process rigor, and product-oriented thinking.

Required Qualifications

10+ years of engineering leadership, including leading distributed teams delivering enterprise-grade software. Deep experience with .NET, Angular, and modern web architectures; Background in healthcare software, particularly claims management or payer-provider integrations. Proven ability to drive execution in complex organizations and deliver against product roadmaps. Experience with SDLC governance, agile engineering, and CI/CD practices. Demonstrated success modernizing legacy platforms and leading convergence initiatives. Strong cross-functional collaboration experience across Product, Operations, and QA. Geographically located within 1 hour drive of Burlington, VT; Tallahassee, FL; Austin, TX; or Atlanta, GA in support of periodic face to face coordination.

Preferred Qualifications

Familiarity with cloud migration and hybrid environments. Experience modernizing applications for SaaS delivery. Previous leadership in PE-backed or growth-stage software companies.

Compensation & Benefits

Target bonus: 15% Performance Incentive Units (PIUs) Remote-first work model with periodic travel Executive-level visibility and opportunity to shape core product delivery

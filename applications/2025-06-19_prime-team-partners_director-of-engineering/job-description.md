About the job

Director of Engineering – Modernization & Cloud Platform

Location: Austin, TX (Hybrid Preferred)

Base Comp: $175-215K, DOE. Must be able to work W2, no sponsorship available

Our healthcare client is seeking a hands-on Director of Engineering to lead the transformation of heir flagsthip EHR and practice management application. This critical role requires a strategic leader who can guide the modernization of a complex legacy Windows application stack, while architecting and delivering a next-generation, cloud-native platform. You’ll be responsible for shaping technical direction, building and mentoring a high-caliber engineering team, and collaborating closely with product, support, and leadership teams to ensure they continue to deliver secure, reliable, and innovative solutions to thousands of providers.

Key Responsibilities:

Modernization Strategy & Execution: Lead the transition of a WinForms/.NET 4.5 monolith into a modern, modular architecture using .NET 8+, WPF, Avalonia, and web technologies (e.g., Vue.js, TypeScript).
New Platform Development: Architect and oversee the development of a new cloud-native product built on a scalable services architecture, integrating modern database, caching, and deployment strategies.
Team Building & Leadership: Hire and lead a growing team including senior Windows developers, UI/UX designers, and QA professionals. Foster a culture of technical excellence, accountability, and continuous improvement.
Process & Quality: Establish and champion engineering best practices around documentation, CI/CD, test automation, and secure software development.
AI-Enhanced Development: Embrace and integrate commercial AI tools (e.g., GitHub Copilot, Claude, Cursor) into engineering workflows to accelerate coding, enhance feature delivery, and boost developer productivity
Cross-functional Collaboration: Partner with support, data transfer, and product teams to align modernization efforts with customer needs and technical feasibility.
Technical Oversight: Ensure architectural decisions support multi-tenant design, observability, security, data protection, and long-term maintainability.

Requirements:

10+ years in software engineering with at least 5 years in technical leadership roles
Deep expertise in Microsoft technologies: .NET Framework/.NET 8, WinForms, WPF, SQL/MySQL/MariaDB
Experience leading modernization of legacy codebases and transitioning to cloud-native architectures
Strong grasp of software design patterns, MVVM, microservices, and frontend-backend decoupling
Track record of hiring, mentoring, and scaling engineering teams
Familiarity with GitHub-based workflows, and Agile methodologies
Passion for improving technical processes, documentation, and developer productivity

Nice to Have:

Experience with Avalonia UI, TypeScript/React, Redis, or Kubernetes
Background in healthcare, especially EHR systems or HIPAA-compliant applications
Hands-on experience with observability tools and security frameworks (e.g., SentinelOne, SonarQube)

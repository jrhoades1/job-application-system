Job Description
About the job

AbendHealth is a profitable and growing insurance-based primary care clinic in New Jersey with 3,000+ active patients. We’re now entering our next phase: building a modern, scalable tech platform that enhances patient care, integrates AI, and positions us for long-term growth.

I'm looking for a Fractional CTO or experienced Technical Advisor to help lead the strategic development of our mobile app —someone who understands the business of healthcare, AI, thinks long-term, and can guide development decisions.

Key Responsibilities:

Guide product/tech decisions and app architecture
Help define MVP scope with purpose-driven features
Vet, hire, and manage dev team
Oversee integrations with tools like Elation (our EHR), Infermedica (AI triage), Google Drive, Doximity/Zoom, etc.
Ensure HIPAA-conscious development (no PHI stored in app)
Advise on future AI assistant development (GPT-based)

Key MVP Features:

White-labeled AI symptom checker
Patient messaging, scheduling, lab access (Elation)
Telehealth integration (Zoom, Doximity, etc.)
Nutrition & fitness video library
AI assistant for patient engagement

Ideal Candidate:

Experience as a fractional CTO, technical advisor, or product-minded senior engineer
Understands HIPAA-conscious product design
Able to think long-term
Excited about healthcare innovation and consumer tech

This is a contracted, paid role with potential for long-term collaboration and equity if aligned. Looking for someone who can start advising immediately on roadmap and MVP planning.

Let’s build something big together.

— Jesse

About ImageAssist

ImageAssist is a fast-growing health tech company modernizing how clinicians capture, manage, and store clinical photographs. Over 200,000,000 clinical photos are taken per year but multiple pain points surrounding capture, organization and sharing persist, all taking away from focusing on the patient relationship and impeding clinical documentation, research & insurance authorization.

Our iOS and web-based platform empowers providers, from solo plastic surgeons to hospital systems. As easy as mobile check deposit, ImageAssist fuses patent-pending SmartFrames™, a seamless HIPAA-compliant storage, and EMR integrations to make ImageAssist a changer for clinicians world-wide.

Incubated at Vanderbilt University Medical Center and endorsed by top plastic surgeons, ImageAssist is used in clinical & research settings daily—and we’re just getting started!

The Role

We’re seeking a hands-on, problem-solving Head of Engineering to be part of our journey to transform patient care by sculpting _ the standard for clinical photography with ImageAssist. _ You’ll be responsible for the end-to-end delivery of ImageAssist across iOS, web, and EMR integrations. A desire to engage in meaningful work to use technology to directly improve patient care is requisite. You’d ideally directly ( or indirectly) manage & maintain the code base repositories, front-end and back end, ensure infrastructure is secure and scalable, and help shape the future of AI-powered features. Second, you’ll collaborate closely with the CEO and founding team, owning the build-out of the engineering team as we scale, contributing to the product roadmap and vision for the product and company.

Job Description

Vice President of Product Engineering

This role is open to fully remote work.

b.well is looking for a VP of Engineering to lead its development efforts in reshaping how people interact with their healthcare. Help reimagine how consumers find and receive healthcare!

b.well is transforming how healthcare consumers find and receive care in a personalized, convenient and affordable manner. Partnering with our customers (health systems, insurance payors, pharmacies and employers) we empower consumers to make informed choices about their family's health using our data platform and our mobile apps.

Currently the b.well technology is in the hands of over 150 million people. Every Samsung phone sold in the U.S. includes the b.well technology and every user of the Walgreens App has b.well technology in their hands.

A great candidate for this role is a strong people manager who is passionate about designing and scaling a successful engineering organization that is enhancing and scaling our internal and external products across desktop and mobile. The person in this role will be an integral part of the senior leadership team, partnering with others on that team to translate our company vision and strategy into engineering strategies that enable us to achieve our purpose at scale.

You will play a vital role in architecting solutions, taking projects from spec to concept, and leading your team to creating high-quality, creative, simple, and elegant solutions. You'll have technical ownership of software product portfolios and their respective engineering teams.

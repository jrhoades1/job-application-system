We’re looking for a seasoned and strategic Senior Director of Engineering to lead our Forward Engineering organization. Your leadership will shape how we scale and serve developers around the world.

In this role, Engineering, you will be responsible for defining and executing the technical strategy for a critical area of our product. You will lead multiple engineering teams, fostering a culture of excellence, collaboration, and continuous improvement. This is a hands-on leadership role where you will balance strategic thinking with direct involvement in technical decisions, ensuring alignment with DigitalOcean's overall business objectives.

What You’ll Be Doing:
Set the long-term vision and technical direction for the Forward Engineering organization.
Drive architecture and strategy for building scalable, reliable infrastructure used by developers worldwide.
Lead multiple teams through senior engineering managers, creating alignment across product, engineering, and design.
Serve as a key voice in company-wide technical decisions, balancing innovation with operational stability.
Build and scale an inclusive, high-performance engineering culture focused on learning, accountability, and delivery.
Partner with executive leadership to align engineering goals with overall business objectives.
What We'll Expect From You:
15+ years of experience in software engineering, with at least 8 years leading large, cross-functional engineering organizations.
Demonstrated success scaling cloud infrastructure products and platforms.
Strong executive communication skills and the ability to influence at the company level.
Deep experience with distributed systems, multi-tenant architectures, and developer platforms.
Proven ability to drive alignment across product, engineering, and go-to-market teams.
Strategic mindset with a bias for action and a track record of delivering results in high-growth environments.
Bachelor's or Master’s degree in Computer Science or a related field.

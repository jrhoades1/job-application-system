About Pathwise

Pathwise is a private equity-backed software as a service (SaaS) growth business currently serving 4,000+ school districts and universities. Founded in 1994, we have team members across both the US and Canada. Our team, which includes former teachers, principals, administrators, and federal program directors, is dedicated to being the leader in providing technology that transforms resource-constrained departments by automating complex tasks, providing real-time visibility, and ensuring seamless communication. We streamline operational processes so schools can focus more time and resources on student outcomes.

As Vice President of Engineering, you will own the strategic direction, execution, and team culture of Pathwise’s engineering organization. You’ll guide distributed engineering teams building next-generation SaaS solutions on Node.js, MySQL/MariaDB, AWS, Vue.js, Flutter, and Redis—with a focus on geospatial technologies, routing, and GIS to transform school transportation and operations. You inspire systems thinking, T-shaped growth, and end-to-end outcome ownership.

Responsibilities

Lead and coach engineering leaders and teams, fostering a culture of continuous learning and systems thinking.
Drive technical vision, execution standards, and delivery of innovative SaaS products.
Guide adoption and evolution of our core stack: Node.js, MySQL/MariaDB, Redis, AWS, Vue.js, Flutter; champion geospatial and GIS innovation.
Set and manage budgets, allocate resources, and oversee hiring/onboarding.
Collaborate with Product and cross-functional executives on strategic roadmaps.
Ensure code quality, delivery cadence, and operational excellence in a cloud-native, multi-tenant environment.
Foster transparency, ownership, and measurable customer impact.
Pursue top talent and mentor teams to embrace outcome-driven, T-shaped growth.

Qualifications and Experience

Bachelor’s or Master’s in CS/Engineering or related; equivalent experience considered.
15+ years in engineering roles, including 7+ leading SaaS/cloud focused teams.
Experience aligning teams and architecture to deliver on aggressive roadmaps.
Familiarity with Pathwise stack: Node.js, MySQL or MariaDB, Redis, AWS, Vue.js, Flutter.
Prior experience with mapping, routing, GPS, GIS, or geospatial data in SaaS solutions (strongly valued).
Engaged, motivational leader with proven change management and organizational growth.
Strong communication, budgeting, stakeholder, and Agile experience.

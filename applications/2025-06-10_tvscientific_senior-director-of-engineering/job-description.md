About tvScientific

tvScientific is the first and only CTV advertising platform purpose-built for performance marketers. We leverage massive data and cutting-edge science to automate and optimize TV advertising to drive business outcomes. Our solution combines media buying, optimization, measurement, and attribution in one, efficient platform. Our platform is built by industry leaders with a long history in programmatic advertising, digital media, and ad verification who have now purpose-built a CTV performance platform advertisers can trust to grow their business.

 

Role Summary

We're hiring a Senior Director of Engineering to lead at the intersection of product vision, technical strategy, and organizational scale. Reporting directly to the VP of Technology, you’ll oversee a 30+ person engineering org through two Directors (Platform and Application Engineering) and take full ownership of architecture, delivery, and team performance.

This is a mission-critical role. You won’t just “manage” engineering:  you’ll shape what we build, how we build it, and how fast we get there. From identity resolution to data governance to in-housing critical technologies, you’ll own cross-functional initiatives that power our growth and differentiation in a fast-evolving ad tech space.

If you thrive in high-stakes, high-ownership environments and are hungry to create technology that defines an entire category, this is your seat at the table.

 

What You'll Do

Align engineering strategy and technical roadmaps with business goals and product priorities
Lead cross-functional execution of key initiatives including identity resolution, data governance, in-housing of core platform capabilities, and so much more.
Own architectural direction across platform infrastructure, core services, and application engineering, ensuring scalability, availability, and performance
Guide the design and evolution of high-scale, cloud-native systems (particularly within AWS environments)
Set and uphold high standards for engineering quality, operational excellence, and code maintainability
Advocate for and lead foundational architectural investments that support long-term growth and flexibility
Ensure systems are designed for compliance with privacy and data protection regulations such as GDPR and CCPA
Partner closely with Product, Commercial, Executive teams to translate business needs into scalable, reliable technical solutions
Communicate clearly, candidly, and frequently across technical and non-technical stakeholders, surfacing risks and ensuring shared alignment
Foster a culture of ownership, transparency, and continuous improvement across the engineering organization

How We'll Define Success

Business-critical engineering initiatives are delivered on-time, with measurable impact
Technical architecture evolves to meet performance, scale, and compliance demands
Engineering team is growing, high-performing, and deeply engaged
Stakeholders view engineering as a strategic partner, not just a delivery function
Cross-functional collaboration is high-trust and high-velocity
Engineering culture reflects our values: ownership, transparency, and excellence

 

You’ll Be Successful in This Role if You Have/Are

10+ years successfully leading high-performing engineering orgs and know how to grow, mentor, and align multiple teams (including Platform Services and Application Software Engineering) through Engineering Directors
You turn ambitious technical roadmaps into real outcomes.  Whether it’s in-housing technology components, extending measurement and attribution systems, or rolling out data governance practices 
You’ve made key architectural calls in cloud-native, high-scale environments (especially on AWS) and know how to design systems that last
You don’t wait for direction; you anticipate risks, manage ambiguity, and keep teams focused on the why behind the work
You bring deep ad tech experience to the table.  Knowledge of SSPs, DSPs, DMPs, OpenRTB, VAST, and identity resolution are tablestakes
You translate engineering trade-offs clearly across Product, Commercial, and Executive teams.  People trust your judgment because you’re transparent, organized, and solution-oriented

 

You May Also Have

A CS or related technical degree
Experience guiding in-house development vs. third-party platform integration decisions
Exposure to privacy-centric ad targeting strategies and evolving measurement practices

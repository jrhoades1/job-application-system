Job Description

Taxwell helps everyday Americans get every tax advantage they deserve by finding credits and deductions they never even knew existed. Our tax preparation software offers easy guidance and ensures your maximum tax refund. We strive to build a team of like-minded experts in both tax and technology who align with our brand purpose, are advocates for our customers and have a fresh, non-traditional approach to the tax industry.

TaxAct is a leading digital tax filing platform which offers customers do-it-yourself digital and downloadable products that are easy-to-use, best-in-class technology, and provide unparalleled customer support. We are a trusted solution for all users including those with complex tax returns. We strive to attract and retain candidates who exemplify our values: performance, perseverance, progress and partnership. TaxAct is a member of the Taxwell family of products. We are an organization of forward thinkers looking to add industry experts to our growing team. 

This role will primarily support TaxAct as the organization helps everyday Americans file their taxes. We seek to build a team of experts in tax and technology who are customer advocates and have the mindset to reimagine the services our customers receive.  

We’re looking for a seasoned Senior Manager, Software Engineering , to lead two foundational teams focused on our pre-auth pages, authentication workflows, and early customer experience. These experiences represent our users’ first interactions with Taxwell, and your leadership will help ensure those interactions are intuitive, trustworthy, and seamless. 

You’ll be managing a team of approximately 15 engineers, including two engineering managers, and will work closely with product, design, and senior leadership to drive key initiatives forward. This is a high-impact role with visibility across the organization—ideal for someone with strong middle management experience who’s looking to expand their influence and scope. As the company scales, so will the opportunity to shape our engineering organization, culture, and strategy. 

At Taxwell, we’re fully bought in on the transformative potential of AI in software development. We're actively transitioning our workflows to incorporate AI-powered tools—from code generation and documentation to automated testing and architecture exploration. You’ll play a key role in helping your team’s experiment, adopt, and scale these tools responsibly balancing innovation with sound engineering judgment. 

Responsibilities** :**

Lead and support two engineering managers and their teams, fostering clarity, accountability, and cohesion across ~15 engineers. 

Mentor managers and senior ICs to help them grow in leadership, ownership, and technical depth. 

Promote a high-performing, inclusive team culture centered on autonomy, alignment, and engineering excellence. 

Encourage thoughtful adoption of AI-enhanced development tools where they improve efficiency, clarity, or confidence. 

Conduct regular 1:1s, performance reviews, and career development conversations. 

Product & Technical Strategy 

Collaborate with product, UX, and business stakeholders to define roadmaps for user-facing systems such as authentication, onboarding, and tax document management. 

Ensure technical decisions are aligned with product priorities and long-term business objectives. 

Drive architecture and platform decisions that promote scalability, maintainability, and developer productivity. 

Identify opportunities to incorporate AI/ML into both the product experience and internal tooling where it adds clear user or developer value. 

Execution & Delivery 

Own the delivery of high-impact features with predictable execution and high quality. 

Establish and improve team practices around planning, estimation, testing, deployment, and monitoring. 

Drive a culture of accountability, transparency, and continuous improvement across the teams. 

Proactively identify and resolve delivery risks or process bottlenecks to keep teams focused and effective. 

Cross-Functional Influence 

Serve as a trusted partner to executive leadership by providing engineering insight into strategic decisions. 

Collaborate with peer managers and tech leads to promote alignment, shared standards, and platform consistency. 

Contribute to broader organizational initiatives around platform modernization, AI-forward development workflows, and continuous improvement. 

Experience  

Qualifications:

8+ years in software engineering, including 5+ years in engineering management (with at least 1–2 years managing other managers and multiple teams). 

Proven success delivering consumer-facing web applications at scale. 

Demonstrated experience operating at the middle management layer—balancing organizational leadership with technical strategy. 

Experience building or overseeing features such as authentication, user onboarding, or account management is a strong plus. 

Previous roles at high-growth or startup-stage companies preferred. 

Technical Acumen 

Familiarity with modern, full-stack technologies—our teams primarily use C#/.NET on the backend and Vue.js on the frontend. 

Experience with similar technologies (e.g., Java, Go, React) is welcome. 

Comfortable guiding teams working in varied stacks; while direct coding isn’t required, you can speak fluently with engineers about their technical decisions. 

Familiarity with CI/CD, automated testing, secure development practices, and the practical use of AI tools in software engineering. 

Leadership & Communication 

Inspiring people leader who can coach managers, influence peers, and communicate effectively with executives. 

A clear and compelling communicator who can translate business needs into engineering goals—and vice versa. 

Organized, strategic thinker who can balance long-term vision with short-term execution. 

Bonus Points  

Experience with tax, fintech, or other highly regulated industries. 

Passion for building and scaling AI-assisted workflows that elevate developer experience and productivity. 

Track record of driving team transformation, modernization, or rapid scaling.

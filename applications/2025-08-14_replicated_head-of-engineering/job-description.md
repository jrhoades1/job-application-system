Job Description

Replicated is a Commercial Software Distribution Platform. Replicated helps software vendors distribute their applications into self-hosted environments like VPC, on-prem, air gap, and more. With a suite of tools ranging from installation, to testing, to licensing and support, Replicated is the best way to operationalize and scale the distribution of Kubernetes applications into any enterprise environment.

Our customers include KNIME, Puppet, Smartbear, BigID, Swimlane, and many other fast-growing enterprise software vendors.

Replicated is committed to cultivating an efficient, respectful workplace. We know that innovation thrives on teams where diverse points of view come together to solve hard problems in ways that are just now possible. As such, we explicitly seek people that bring diverse life experiences, diverse educational backgrounds, diverse cultures, and diverse work experiences.

We are fully remote and plan to stay that way! We're open to any state in the US. In addition, for some roles, we're open to candidates in Canada, the UK, Australia, and New Zealand.

Replicated is hiring a hands-on engineering leader to join our growing team!

Our development group is organized in the form of pods of full stack teams with clear areas of ownership within our product ecosystem. We encourage and support teams to own their product metrics and focus on consistently delivering value to our customers.

We are looking for a Head of Engineering who will spend time coding and building while also empowering the team to deliver on their key priorities. This is a technical leadership role where you'll be writing code regularly alongside managing resources, reviewing metrics, and ensuring we deliver quality products with speed and efficiency. A big part of joining us at this stage is to help contribute to the advancement of our product through both your direct technical contributions and leadership. We believe in continuous improvement along with a growth mindset, we support each other to learn and grow so we can continue to have the best engineering and team culture.

We strive for a culture of impact, this means that it is important for everyone to focus on delivering results. These results are not based on quantity, but on the true value of our product and to our customers. Each team is responsible for ownership of their product throughout its full life cycle.

The Vision for Success:

Be hands-on with coding and architecture, driving both velocity and technical excellence through direct contribution

Drive product vision and technical strategy using your engineering expertise and market understanding

Partner closely with Product while also leveraging your technical background to influence and shape product direction

Champion AI adoption and integration within engineering practices, leveraging AI tools to enhance productivity and product capabilities

Assess incoming customer issues and personally contribute to solving complex technical challenges

People Leadership:

Lead a team of technical leads and engineers, focusing on growth, development, and delivery

Hire, support and contribute to the growth of the Engineering organization

Startup Agility:

Thrive in a fast-paced startup environment where priorities shift and change frequently

Adapt quickly to new technical challenges, market feedback, and evolving customer needs

Work cross functionally with other groups needed to successfully implement initiatives in a dynamic, ever-changing landscape

Develop a deep understanding and clearly communicate the problems and opportunities of our users and customers; advocating for and driving forward solutions with startup speed and flexibility

Your Journey With Us:

In your first 30 days: Your goal is to dive deep into both the technical architecture and the codebase while learning about the company, team and product. You will complete hands-on technical training with the product, complete the onboarding checklist, and meet with team members from across the company. Expect to start contributing code and understanding current technical initiatives.

In your first 60 days: Begin taking on coding responsibilities for key projects while outlining how to break them down into iterative deliverables. Lead weekly planning and standups, supporting your engineers while contributing technically to solutions. Evaluate current AI tool usage and identify opportunities to enhance engineering productivity through AI adoption. Partner with our CTO and expand your partnership to the wider product and engineering org. Use your technical insights to review metrics and work with senior leadership on success indicators.

In your first 90 days: With full context, you should have full ownership of both engineering delivery and technical direction. Drive career development conversations with engineers while maintaining your active coding contributions. Develop and implement an AI strategy for the engineering team, including tool adoption, process improvements, and potential product enhancements. Own engineering deliverables and actively participate in defining technical strategy and building viable solutions. Be prepared to pivot and adapt as business needs and market conditions evolve.

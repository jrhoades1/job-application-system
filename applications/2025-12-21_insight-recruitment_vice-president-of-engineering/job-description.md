About the job VP of Engineering

Remote (US)

Insight are partnered with an autonomy start-up who need a people-first VP of Engineering who excels at developing talent and creating the structure for engineers to do their best work.

If you thrive in fast-paced environments and want to own the full employee lifecycle - from whiteboarding team structures to scaling a distributed organization - this is for you.

You’ll partner closely with the CTO and CEO to ensure people and processes scale effectively, mentoring tech leads and building a culture of accountability and impact.

What you need:

10+ years of engineering experience 3+ years managing senior-level engineers Proven ability to scale high-performing technical teams within a startup environment Experience implementing performance systems, leveling frameworks, and career paths Excellent communication and the ability to earn trust across both technical and business stakeholders

While not required, experience in robotics, autonomous systems, or hardware/software integration is a major plus.

Ready to make an impact? Apply to learn more.

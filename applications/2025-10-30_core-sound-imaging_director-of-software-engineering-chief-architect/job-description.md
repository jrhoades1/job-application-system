Job Description
About the job

The Director of Software Engineering & Chief Architect will play a dual role in shaping both the technical strategy and the execution of software delivery. This leader will oversee engineering teams while serving as the principal architect responsible for guiding the design, scalability, and performance of our next-generation SaaS healthcare platform. Will be responsible for combining strong technical depth with organizational leadership, aligning architecture with business goals, ensuring engineering excellence, and driving a culture of innovation, accountability, and collaboration.

Key Duties: (Duties are illustrative and not inclusive and may vary.)

Engineering Leadership

Lead, mentor, and grow multiple engineering teams across backend, frontend, DevOps, and QA disciplines.
Drive best practices in software engineering, including agile delivery, CI/CD, code quality, and test automation.
Foster a culture of ownership, transparency, and continuous improvement.
Partner with Product Management and UX to deliver high-quality, customer-focused solutions on time and at scale.

Architectural Strategy

Define and maintain the company’s software architecture roadmap, ensuring scalability, security, interoperability, and regulatory compliance (HIPAA, HITRUST, etc.).
Guide the design of cloud-native, microservices-based solutions that support rapid feature development and high availability.
Evaluate and implement emerging technologies to keep the platform future-ready and competitive.
Champion data architecture that supports analytics, interoperability (FHIR, HL7), and secure data exchange.

Cross-Functional Impact

Collaborate with executive leadership to align technical investments with business strategy.
Partner with Compliance, Security, and Infrastructure teams to ensure adherence to healthcare regulatory requirements.
Represent engineering and architecture perspectives in strategic planning, customer discussions, and industry forums.

Qualifications:

A bachelor’s degree in computer science, software engineering, information technology or related field, and five plus years of experience to successfully perform the essential duties of the job listed above and the additional requirements listed below:

7+ years of progressive experience in software engineering, with at least 5 years in senior leadership roles.
Proven track record leading large-scale SaaS software development in healthcare or similarly regulated industries.
Deep expertise in modern architectures (cloud-native, microservices, event-driven, API-first).
Hands-on experience with cloud platforms (AWS, Azure, or GCP).
Exceptional leadership, communication, and stakeholder management skills.
Experience writing clean codes.
Experience in driving innovation with AI tools to rapidly deliver product roadmaps.
3+ years managing a development team utilizing SCRUM methodology.
Excellent teamwork skills.
Proven ability to influence cross-functional teams without formal authority.
Excellent communication skills - ability to interface and influence at multiple organization levels.
Ability to learn and apply modern technologies quickly.
Excellent problem-solving abilities.
Ability to work both independently and as part of a team.
Medical imaging knowledge a plus.

About the Company

Studycast is a fast-growing provider of a cloud-native platform for viewing, analyzing, and securely sharing diagnostic-quality images to reduce provider and patient costs while improving treatment protocols and overall quality of care. Studycast’s primary product is a radiology Picture Archiving & Communication System (PACS) that is differentiated in its breadth of workflow management tools, extensibility across specialties and care settings, and configurability across imaging modalities and hardware providers. Studycast is a PSG portfolio company. As we scale, we seek a strategic and hands-on VP of Finance to lead and build a world-class finance team and infrastructure that can support our growth trajectory.

About PSG

PSG is a growth equity firm that partners with software and technology-enabled service companies to help them navigate transformational growth, capitalize on strategic opportunities, and build strong teams. Having backed more than 115 companies and facilitated over 420 add-on acquisitions, we bring extensive investment experience, deep expertise in software and technology, and a firm commitment to collaborating with management teams. Founded in 2014, PSG has since raised over $14 billion in capital and has a team of more than 150 investment professionals.

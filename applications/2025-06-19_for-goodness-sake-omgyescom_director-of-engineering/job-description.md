Job Description
About the job

About OMGYES.COM

What if you could use your engineering expertise to increase intimacy, pleasure and joy? Sounds pretty lofty, right?

The company we’ve created measurably and significantly has exactly those impacts.

Our flagship product is a digital sex-education platform called OMGYES.com. We conduct research with tens of thousands of women, ages 18-95, and distill their wisdom about sex and intimacy into a web experience with over a million users. OMGYES gives people clarity about what they like and the confidence to make it happen solo and with partners.

The Role

As the Director of Engineering, you will own the architecture for our platform, collaborate with cross-functional teams in the company to ensure end-to-end strategy and execution and drive execution to achieve business goals. You are passionate about product craftsmanship and care about pixel-perfect design in our product's visual presentation. You have strong judgment to make pragmatic technical decisions, balancing the need for long-term code health and flexibility with the urgency of business objectives. You will lead a team of senior engineers, providing direct management and oversight to ensure they are successful. In this role, you will need to be both hands-on, owning your own initiatives and delivering code, as well as provide engineering leadership and partnership to the executive team.

What you'll do

Design, develop, deploy, and maintain frontend tools, backend services and infrastructure that enable and scale our platform
Learn, adopt, and champion best practices and design patterns, helping to shape the engineering culture of our organization
Own projects end-to-end, effectively communicating with leadership and other stakeholders to deliver high-quality, scalable code
Work in a cloud-native tech stack: AWS, Cloudflare, PostgreSQL
Lead and develop a small team of ambitious engineers to continuously experiment, review, iterate, and deliver on new product objectives and quarterly goals. Drive hiring and vendor selections when needed.
Lead code reviews, troubleshoot system issues, and safeguard our code base and product integrity
Manage the QA team and conduct comprehensive testing, including unit, system, and regression testing, to ensure product reliability
Own the management of engineering tools and systems and their associated costs, including selecting new tooling when needed
Partner with product management, design and executive leadership to represent engineering in planning our quarterly roadmap, building and communicating the strategic technical roadmap and driving team capacity
Lead agile ceremonies such as sprint planning, daily standup, demos and retrospectives.
Serve as a technical leader and subject matter expert, educating senior stakeholders on emerging trends and the strategic value of the work

What You'll Need

Proven Leadership & Management: 5+ years of direct management experience leading, mentoring, and scaling high-performing engineering teams. Demonstrated ability to foster a positive and productive engineering culture.
Technical Strategy & Vision: Experience developing and executing long-term technical roadmaps aligned with business objectives. Ability to evaluate and champion new technologies and architectural patterns to drive innovation and scalability.
Architectural Expertise: Deep understanding of modern web architectures, including frontend frameworks (like React/NextJS), backend services, cloud-native infrastructure (AWS preferred), and database systems (like PostgreSQL). Proven ability to design, develop, and maintain scalable, reliable systems.
Hands-On Technical Proficiency: Significant experience (10+ years) in web development with continued ability and willingness to contribute to the codebase, lead by example, and guide technical decisions at a detailed level. Familiarity with modern development practices including IaC (e.g., CDK, Terraform), CI/CD, automated testing, and API design (e.g., GraphQL).
Cross-Functional Collaboration & Communication: Excellent communication skills with the ability to effectively partner with product management, design, and executive leadership to translate business needs into technical solutions. Experience representing engineering perspectives in strategic planning.
Operational Excellence: Experience managing engineering operations, including QA processes, agile methodologies, system monitoring, and potentially vendor/tooling selection and budget management.
Problem Solving & Execution: Strong analytical and problem-solving skills with a track record of driving projects end-to-end and delivering high-quality results in a dynamic environment.

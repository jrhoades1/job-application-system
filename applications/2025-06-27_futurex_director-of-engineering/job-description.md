Job Description
About the job

🧠 Director of Engineering

📍 Fully Remote, USA Based

💵 $275K Base + Equity

Join a mission-driven startup transforming how hospitals and health systems capture value through intelligent automation. We’re building clinical-grade, AI-powered products that unlock millions in reimbursement and improve care delivery — without adding burden to frontline teams. As our Director of Engineering , you'll play a foundational role in scaling both the product and the team behind it.

_ What You’ll Do _

Lead and grow a high-performing, cross-functional engineering team across frontend, backend, and data science disciplines
Own engineering strategy, execution, and delivery across our AI-driven SaaS platform
Partner closely with product, clinical, and go-to-market leaders to align technical initiatives with business outcomes
Drive architectural decisions, balancing speed, scalability, and reliability
Foster an inclusive, curious, and high-accountability engineering culture rooted in mentorship, ownership, and velocity
Roll up your sleeves when needed — whether it’s reviewing architecture proposals, debugging tricky edge cases, or unblocking teams
Implement metrics and processes to ensure consistent, high-quality delivery and system performance

_ You Might Be a Fit If You _

Have 8–12+ years of software engineering experience, with 5+ years in engineering leadership roles
Have built and scaled engineering teams in fast-paced startup environments
Have experience shipping production systems in healthcare, machine learning, or other regulated industries (HIPAA familiarity a plus)
Are comfortable working across the stack — we use TypeScript, Python, AWS, Postgres
Are outcome-oriented and excited to work in an environment where clinical, financial, and operational impact matters
Care deeply about people and are energized by coaching, leveling-up engineers, and building inclusive teams

_ Why This Role _

This is a rare opportunity to lead engineering at a well-funded startup solving real problems in healthcare using world-class technology. You'll work with thoughtful, mission-driven teammates who believe in doing well by doing good — and who are deeply motivated by the lives we help improve through our work.

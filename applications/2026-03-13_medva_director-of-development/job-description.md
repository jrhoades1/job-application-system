## About the Company

Our client is transforming how healthcare practices operate by connecting doctors, dentists, orthodontists, and other healthcare providers across the United States with highly skilled remote professionals. The organization supports more than 900 client organizations and over 2,500 virtual assistants, helping practices streamline operations and focus on delivering exceptional patient care.

As the company continues to grow, it is investing in building a proprietary technology platform to support the scale and complexity of its operations.

## The Opportunity

Our client is seeking a hands-on Director of Development to lead the creation of its next-generation platform.

This proprietary portal will serve as the operational hub connecting clients, remote professionals, and internal teams. The platform will support workflow management, client and workforce matching, productivity tracking, billing operations, and other systems that power the company's shared services model.

This is a foundational engineering leadership role. The Director of Development will be Employee #1 on the R&D team, responsible for architecting, building, and launching the platform from the ground up.

This role is primarily a senior engineering position (approximately 80% hands-on development and architecture) with 20% leadership responsibilities, including building and managing a small engineering team of approximately 3-4 developers as the platform evolves.

Reporting to the VP of IT, this role will act as the technical owner of the platform, responsible for defining the architecture, building the initial system, and guiding its evolution as the engineering team grows.

The ideal candidate has experience building platforms from concept to production, not simply maintaining or modernizing legacy systems.

## What You'll Do

### Platform Architecture & Development
- Architect and build the company's proprietary operations platform from concept to production
- Write the initial production code and establish the technical foundation for the system
- Select the technology stack and design system architecture
- Build scalable backend services, APIs, and data pipelines
- Design and manage databases (SQL and/or NoSQL)
- Implement CI/CD pipelines and development infrastructure
- Collaborate on frontend experiences and system workflows
- Leverage AWS and cloud-native services to ensure scalability and reliability

### Platform Capabilities
The platform will support a high-volume operational environment that includes:
- Matching clients with remote professionals
- Managing productivity and performance tracking
- Supporting billing workflows for clients and payments to contractors (1099)
- Managing large volumes of operational and performance data
- Supporting a shared services operational model for healthcare practices

### Security & Compliance
- Architect the platform with security and compliance built in from the start
- Support frameworks such as HIPAA, SOC 2, and secure financial workflows
- Establish development standards and secure infrastructure protocols

### Engineering Excellence
- Establish development standards including code reviews, testing frameworks, and documentation
- Ensure the platform is scalable, maintainable, and reliable
- Promote strong engineering best practices across the organization

### Team Leadership
- Recruit and lead a distributed engineering team of approximately 3-4 developers
- Help define hiring needs, evaluate candidates, and build the engineering organization
- Mentor engineers through architecture discussions, code reviews, and technical problem-solving
- Gradually evolve the team structure as the platform and company continue to scale

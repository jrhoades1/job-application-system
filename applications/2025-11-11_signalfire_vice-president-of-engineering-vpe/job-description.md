Job Description
Join SignalFire’s Talent Network for Vice President of Engineering (VP) Roles at VC-Backed Startups

At SignalFire , we partner with top early-stage startups that are shaping the future of technology. Our portfolio spans 200+ innovative companies across AI, cybersecurity, healthtech, fintech, developer tools, and enterprise SaaS.

We’re looking to connect with exceptional Vice Presidents of Engineering who are excited about leading and scaling engineering teams, driving technical strategy, and aligning technology with business growth at high-growth startups. By joining SignalFire’s Talent Network, your profile will be shared with our portfolio companies, giving you visibility into exclusive early-stage opportunities that may not be publicly listed.

💡 This is not an application for a specific job. Instead, this is a way to get on the radar of VC-backed startups that are actively hiring AI/ML talent. If you have any questions, please direct inquiries to talentnetwork@signalfire.com.

Who Should Join?

We’re looking for engineering executives who are:
✔ Passionate about scaling engineering organizations and delivering high-impact technical solutions
✔ Experienced in building world-class engineering teams and optimizing engineering execution
✔ Excited to work closely with founders and executives to shape company strategy

Typical Roles & Responsibilities

Lead and scale engineering teams, hiring and developing top technical talent

Define and execute engineering strategy, ensuring alignment with company goals

Drive technical vision, product architecture, and infrastructure scalability

Oversee engineering operations, delivery timelines, and process improvements

Foster a culture of high performance, collaboration, and innovation

Partner with product, design, and go-to-market teams to drive business impact

Implement best practices in software development, security, and DevOps

Manage budgets, resource planning, and technical roadmaps

Ensure engineering processes enable fast iteration while maintaining quality and reliability

Common Qualifications

While each startup has its own hiring criteria, many VP of Engineering roles in our network look for:

12+ years of experience in software engineering, with 5+ years in senior leadership roles

Proven track record of scaling engineering teams from startup to growth stage

Experience leading distributed teams and cross-functional collaboration

Deep expertise in technical architecture, software development methodologies, and cloud infrastructure

Strong understanding of Agile methodologies, product development cycles, and engineering execution frameworks

Ability to align engineering initiatives with business objectives and revenue growth

Experience working with VC-backed startups or high-growth technology companies

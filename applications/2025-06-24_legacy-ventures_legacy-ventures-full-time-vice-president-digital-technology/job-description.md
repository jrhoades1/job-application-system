Job Description

About Us Legacy Ventures is a leading private equity firm specializing in investments across Business Services, Consumer, Healthcare Services, Industrials & Manufacturing, and real estate. With a proven track record of success, Legacy Ventures partners with visionary entrepreneurs and management teams to drive growth and create long-term value for stakeholders.

Job Description Legacy Ventures is seeking a motivated, talented, and experienced Vice President of Digital for our portfolio company who is technical and has a passion for delivering exceptional digital experiences for enterprise-level clients nationally. The ideal candidate has a track record of leading development teams in the execution of enterprise-level digital experiences from inception to deployment.

Leading a talented team of engineers and digital marketers, you’ll be responsible for delivering first-class digital strategies, experiences and analytics to a wide variety of clients across industries. You will lead strategic and technical decisions while closely managing existing development projects to ensure successful timely delivery and demonstrable results.

This is a hands-on role too – with overall responsibility for delivery, the right candidate would be excited to get involved with new business and existing client strategy presentations, as well as manage the day-to-day execution of all aspects of our digital services with insight into SEO, web development, UI/UX optimization, tracking, custom reporting, advertising, social media, and more. The right person will have a strategic vision along with a pragmatic “get things done” attitude to their role.

Reporting to the President of the company, you’ll be a key member of the Management team with input into agency direction and strategy, and you’ll bring extensive digital marketing and operations knowledge to help drive impactful decisions. The right candidate would ideally be operating in a similar broad-based role and will be a talented digital marketer, capable of inspiring and motivating those around you as the agency looks to progress.

In addition, you’ll be the driving force behind our agency’s own digital strategy, helping to define, shape and implement that strategy as part of the executive management team. You’ll be the go-to person and authority on all things digital and will gain satisfaction from sharing knowledge and expertise with others.

This exciting opportunity will allow you to apply your considerable digital experience and management skills in challenging and varied environments and industry verticals, as we continue to establish ourselves as one of the premier national agencies.

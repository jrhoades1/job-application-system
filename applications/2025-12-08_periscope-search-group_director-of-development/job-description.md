Job Description

About the job Our client is a fintech technology provider and they are seeking a Director of Development to lead their global software engineering team.

Location: South Florida

Hybrid / 4x days in-office

This is a high-impact, hands-on leadership role focused on managing a distributed engineering team, scaling systems, and driving the technical roadmap for a global trading platform. The Director will report to the COO and play a critical role in shaping the architecture, performance, and growth of the company’s core trading technologies.

Key Responsibilities

Lead and scale a cross-functional engineering department (backend, frontend, QA, DevOps) Define and implement the technical roadmap aligned with business strategy Architect scalable, real-time trading systems and platform integrations Maintain technical excellence via best practices in CI/CD, testing, and code quality Partner with cross-functional teams in Product, Risk, and Operations Oversee system performance, reliability, and production readiness Remain hands-on: coding, reviewing architecture, and mentoring developers Hire and develop top engineering talent across multiple time zones

Requirements

10+ years in software development, including 5+ years in a leadership role Experience in fintech, trading, or SaaS environments Expertise in C#, .NET, Node.js, React, and modern cloud architectures Hands-on experience with MongoDB/MySQL, Redis, and AWS or Azure Familiarity with online brokerage or trading platforms preferred Proven success leading distributed teams and scaling technology infrastructure Strong leadership and communication skills with a hands-on, execution-first mindset

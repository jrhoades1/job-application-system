Job Description
About the job

Job Opportunity: Head of Data Platform

Velocity Tech has partnered with an innovative leader in enterprise search and AI-driven information retrieval to hire a Head of Data Platform. This role is pivotal in shaping the company’s data platform strategy, scaling core infrastructure, and building a high-performing engineering team. You will lead efforts to design, develop, and deploy cutting-edge data and AI-driven systems that empower organizations with fast, secure, and intelligent access to information across industries.

Key Responsibilities

Data Platform Strategy & Leadership

Define and execute the roadmap for a scalable, secure, and resilient data platform.
Drive architectural decisions around data pipelines, APIs, storage, and integrations for enterprise-scale solutions.
Ensure best practices in data governance, security, and compliance.

Team Building & Management

Build, lead, and mentor a multidisciplinary engineering team (backend, frontend, and platform engineers).
Oversee hiring, collaborating closely with both agencies and internal recruitment teams to attract and retain top talent.
Foster a culture of innovation, collaboration, and continuous improvement.

Technical Excellence

Lead the design and development of robust backend systems, APIs, and services powering enterprise data solutions.
Oversee frontend engineering efforts to ensure intuitive and performant user interfaces for enterprise clients.
Work hands-on with technologies spanning NLP, Generative AI, Python, Spark, and cloud platforms to enable advanced search and retrieval capabilities.
Scale infrastructure for high-volume, low-latency workloads, ensuring reliability and performance.

Stakeholder Collaboration

Partner with product, research, and engineering teams to align technical delivery with business goals.
Engage with clients and stakeholders, understanding requirements and translating them into platform capabilities.
Provide technical thought leadership and contribute to the overall data strategy.

Required Qualifications

Bachelor’s or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Data Engineering, or related field.
7+ years of experience in data engineering, platform engineering, or ML/AI systems.
Proven experience building and leading engineering teams , including responsibility for hiring and scaling through both internal and external recruitment channels.
Expertise in Python and experience with large-scale data processing frameworks (e.g., Spark, Databricks).
Strong background in NLP/AI systems and familiarity with LLMs and Generative AI.
Hands-on knowledge of cloud environments (Azure, AWS, GCP), CI/CD, and containerization (Docker, Kubernetes).
Strong communication skills with the ability to influence at both technical and executive levels.

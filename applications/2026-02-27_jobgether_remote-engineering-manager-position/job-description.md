This position is posted by Jobgether on behalf of a partner company. We are currently looking for a Data Engineering Lead - REMOTE. This role is crucial as we build a scalable data platform to manage complex simulation data. By leveraging state-of-the-art cloud technology, the Data Engineering Lead will design infrastructure that transforms high-volume simulation outputs into reliable datasets. The impact of this position extends to enhancing analytics capabilities and improving customer insights, ultimately shaping the future of operational simulations. Your contributions will enable better decision-making by leaders in critical scenarios. Join us in this exciting journey of innovation and growth.
Accountabilities
Architect our AWS-based data warehouse and ingestion pipelines
Transform high-volume simulation outputs into clean, trusted datasets
Establish schema standards and data contracts with engineering
Enable scenario replay, comparison, and traceability
Support analytics and AI workflows across the platform
Hire and develop a high-performing data engineering team
Requirements
5+ years of professional experience as a Data Engineer or Data Platform Engineer
Deep experience building production-grade batch and streaming pipelines
Proficiency in Python, SQL, and at least one data processing framework
Experience with cloud-based data infrastructure (AWS, GCP, or Azure)
Experience designing schemas and modeling complex systems
Experience leading or mentoring engineers
Clear, concise written and verbal communication skills
Ability to reason about data as a product, not a byproduct
Benefits
Work in an all-remote company environment
Opportunity to lead a greenfield data engineering function
Collaborate with a diverse and experienced team
Competitive compensation and benefits package
Flexible working hours and location independence
Growth opportunities in a fast-paced and innovative industry

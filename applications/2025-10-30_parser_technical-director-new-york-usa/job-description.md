Job Description
About the job

Parser partners with companies of all sizes to solve business challenges through technology. Our agile, self-managed teams collaborate closely with clients to deliver innovative solutions. Working with us means joining a community of highly skilled professionals, continuously growing and sharing knowledge.

As a Technical Director , you will drive technical excellence, ensure outstanding project delivery, and foster strong client relationships to support account growth. You will act as a trusted technical advisor, leading teams, managing engagements, and identifying new business opportunities.

Key Responsibilities

Technical Leadership: Oversee architecture, design, and implementation to ensure best-in-class solutions.
Client Growth & Strategy: Identify opportunities, propose technical solutions, and enhance Parser’s presence within client organizations.
Relationship Management: Build trust with key stakeholders, ensuring satisfaction and long-term engagement.
Team Leadership: Lead, mentor, and manage technical teams, ensuring smooth onboarding, performance optimization, and career development.
Technical Excellence: Stay ahead of industry trends, contribute to Parser’s intellectual property, and represent the company at conferences.
Governance & Pre-sales: Define technical standards, conduct client workshops, generate proposals, and develop Proof of Concepts (POCs) on cloud platforms.
Business Development: Act as the first technical presence in new client engagements, leading farming and hunting initiatives.

What You Bring

15+ years in software development , with 8+ years leading engineering teams.
4+ years as a Technical or Engineering Director in software consulting.
Expertise in modern architectures (microservices, event-driven, cloud-based), AI/ML and data-intensive solutions.
Strong programming skills in at least two major languages (e.g., React, Angular, Python, Java, C#, Node.js, .NET).
Deep knowledge of SQL, NoSQL, and vector databases.
Experience with AWS, GCP, or Azure and developing POCs on cloud platforms.
Proficiency in SDLC best practices , including testing, CI/CD, and documentation.
Technical sales and pre-sales experience , with a track record of growing client accounts.
Public speaking experience and ability to represent Parser at conferences.
Industry expertise in finance or healthcare is preferred.
Fluent English required; Spanish is a plus.

Location: Must be based in or near New York City, USA, with occasional client travel.

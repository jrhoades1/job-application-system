## About Rezilient Health

Rezilient Health is reimagining primary care with a hybrid care model combining technology-enabled clinics and virtual care. The company partners with health systems and payers to deliver high-quality, affordable primary and multispecialty care. Backed by venture funding, Rezilient is scaling nationally.

## Role Summary

The VP of Engineering will lead all engineering efforts, owning the technical roadmap and delivery of Rezilient's healthcare platform. This is a senior leadership role reporting to the CEO/CTO, responsible for building and scaling the engineering team, driving platform architecture, and ensuring the technology enables Rezilient's hybrid care model.

## Requirements

### Must-Have
- 10+ years of software engineering experience, with 5+ years in engineering leadership (VP, Director, or Head of Engineering)
- Experience building and scaling engineering teams (20+ engineers)
- Track record of shipping production healthcare software (EHR integrations, patient-facing applications)
- Deep familiarity with healthcare interoperability standards (HL7, FHIR)
- Experience with HIPAA compliance and healthcare security frameworks (SOC2, HITRUST)
- Strong background in cloud infrastructure (AWS preferred)
- Experience with modern web technologies (Node.js, React, or similar)
- Series A–C startup experience; comfortable with ambiguity and rapid iteration

### Nice-to-Have
- Experience integrating AI/ML into healthcare workflows
- Familiarity with value-based care models and payer partnerships
- Experience with telemedicine/virtual care platforms
- Background in platform engineering and API-first architecture
- M&A technical due diligence experience

## Responsibilities
- Own the technical roadmap and align it with business objectives
- Build, mentor, and scale a high-performing engineering team
- Drive architecture decisions for scalability, reliability, and security
- Ensure HIPAA compliance and maintain SOC2/HITRUST certifications
- Partner with product, clinical, and operations teams
- Manage engineering budget and vendor relationships
- Establish engineering culture, processes, and best practices
- Report to CEO/CTO on engineering progress and technical strategy

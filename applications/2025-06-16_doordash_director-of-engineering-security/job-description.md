Job Description

About the Team

At DoorDash we’re building the industry’s most scalable and reliable delivery network to support our three-sided marketplace of consumers, merchants, and dashers. Security is paramount to the success of our business, and DoorDash Security aspires to be the world’s best security team. We are committed to protecting our people, partners, customers, and technologies with robust safeguards and unwavering vigilance. 

About the Role

We’re looking for a Director of Engineering, Security to lead our Security Engineering and Security Enablement teams. Our Security Engineering team comprises software engineers building and operating the foundational security infrastructure and controls underlying all our products and services. Our Security Enablement team comprises security engineers who are experts in our business, acting as trusted security partners to our engineering, product, and design teams to solve security problems across business verticals.

We are looking for an exceptional technical software engineering and security leader to evolve our systems and infrastructure such that security is always on by default and provided as a service in our production environments, abstracting security away from our DoorDash engineers, so that they can single mindedly focus on growing our business and delighting our customers.

This role will be based in the San Francisco Bay Area or Seattle, and report to our Chief Information Security Officer and be part of the security leadership team at DoorDash.

You’re excited about this opportunity because you will…

Deliver vision and strategy for a builder-first security organization, delivering security solutions at scale and owning the outcomes.

Define and scale a global Security Partner organization that will not only focus on securing our products and services, but also innovate in security to reduce customer friction, increase engineering velocity, and unlock new business opportunities.
Bring software engineering expertise in building and operating high scale systems with a focus on reliability, scalability, efficiency and ease of use.
Build, sustain, and grow a combined software engineering and security engineering organization to address the growing needs of DoorDash.
We’re excited about you because…

_You have an advanced degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Information Security, or a related discipline._You have 15+ years experience in an engineering management role spanning software engineering and security engineering. 

You have expertise in cloud infrastructure engineering, developer experience, platform engineering, and information security.
You maintain a high bar for engineering operational excellence, and have built and operated mission-critical, reliable, scalable, and highly available engineering systems
You have a track record of growing Software Engineers, Engineering Managers, Security Engineers, and Security Managers from entry-level to senior principal engineer and senior manager levels
You are an exceptional partner to engineering teams, and leverage your deep cross-functional technical expertise to earn trust and forge durable partnerships in a fast moving environment.

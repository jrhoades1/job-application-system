# Head of Tech & Development — Bella Connections

**Company:** Bella Connections
**Location:** Remote (USA, Canada, UK, France preferred) — CT-ET and EU timezones
**Compensation:** $160,000 - $200,000 + Performance Bonus
**Experience:** 6-9 Years

## About the Role

This position leads the technical operations for a multi-brand DTC ecommerce portfolio focused on health & wellness. The successful candidate will oversee funnels, conversion optimization, and analytics while managing a development team.

## Key Responsibilities

### Team Leadership
- Manage and grow a 6-person development team
- Hire and retain top technical talent

### Optimization
- Own performance metrics including page speed and funnel stability
- Improve tracking reliability and analytics accuracy

### Systems & Automation
- Implement structured development processes (sprints, QA, deployment)
- Build internal tools automating workflows and reducing errors
- Focus on split testing frequency as a measurable KPI

### Technical Infrastructure
- Create standardized templates for funnels and pages
- Design architecture supporting multiple brands at scale
- Establish environment enabling rapid CRO testing

### Analysis & Reporting
- Provide weekly performance metrics and updates
- Own BI reporting using customized analytics tools
- Manage pixel and tag tracking systems

### Security Leadership
- Oversee device and information security
- Influence company-wide security policies

## Must-Have Qualifications

- Proven DTC/ecommerce operational experience
- Deep understanding of funnels, CRO, attribution, and analytics
- Engineering leadership and structured process management experience
- Business-to-technical translation ability
- High ownership mindset with revenue accountability
- Quick problem-solving with high technical standards
- **Essential:** Checkout Champ experience

## Bonus Qualifications

- GA4, pixel tracking, and event-based architecture expertise
- Multi-brand management experience
- High-growth environment background
- Front-end execution capabilities
- Internal dashboard or reporting tool development
- Attribution and automation systems exposure

## 90-Day Goals

"Page load speed, tracking accuracy, and funnel stability are materially improved, standardized funnel and page templates are live, and CRO testing is running consistently."

Responsibilities Accelerate Ascension’s journey to transform healthcare for those we serve and those who serve by delivering brand-wide digital experiences powered by people, design, data, AI, and technology.

You will be a change agent in the software engineering organization, responsible for engineering delivery with quality and speed across multiple digital products, while driving operational excellence. In addition to core engineering leadership, this role will lead the adoption, governance, and scaling of AI-enabled capabilities (including generative AI, and intelligent automation) across digital products and platforms. Success in this role ensures high-quality technical delivery, responsible AI implementation, and a culture of continuous innovation and engineering excellence.

Responsibilities Engineering Leadership & Delivery

Lead engineering teams for multiple strategic digital products, accountable for planning, delivery, quality, reliability, and operations. Partner closely with Product, Experience, Architecture, Data, Security, and Program leadership to drive outcomes, roadmaps, and release timelines. Own technical design decisions, including functional and non-functional requirements such as scalability, security, performance, and resiliency. Communicate engineering and AI vision, strategy, delivery plans, and progress to executive and senior stakeholders. AI & Intelligent Systems Leadership

Define and execute the AI engineering strategy for digital products, aligned with Ascension’s enterprise AI, data, privacy, and security strategies. Lead the design, development, and productionization of AI-enabled solutions, leveraging: Generative AI (LLMs, copilots, conversational interfaces) Intelligent search, personalization, and recommendations Workflow automation and decision support Establish best practices for LLMOps, including model lifecycle management, prompt engineering, evaluation, monitoring, and retraining. Ensure responsible AI practices, including explainability, bias mitigation, data privacy, regulatory compliance (HIPAA, healthcare AI guidance), and human-in-the-loop controls. Evaluate and govern AI platforms, tools, and vendors (cloud AI services, open-source frameworks, model providers). Engineering Excellence & Modern Practices

Lead by example in building high-quality, maintainable, secure, and performant systems using cloud-native and AI-enabled architectures. Drive shift-left practices across testing, security, observability, performance, and AI quality evaluation. Foster strong peer review, architecture review, and model review cultures. Champion reuse of shared platforms, APIs, data products, and AI components. Operational Excellence & DevSecOps

Embed DevSecOps, Infrastructure as Code, CI/CD, and automated quality gates across software and AI pipelines. Drive a strong SRE and production ownership mindset, including incident response, reliability engineering, and cost optimization. Implement metrics-driven product and AI health reporting (availability, latency, quality, model performance, cost, and adoption). Thought Leadership & Innovation

Contribute to Ascension GMDx–wide engineering and AI strategy, standards, frameworks, and reference architectures. Stay current with emerging trends in software engineering, AI, and healthcare technology, translating them into practical solutions. Promote experimentation, rapid prototyping, and data-informed decision making. Represent Ascension engineering in internal forums and external communities through presentations, demos, and knowledge sharing. Talent, Culture & Change Leadership

Build, mentor, and grow high-performing teams of engineering leaders, software engineers, and AI engineers. Upskill teams in AI literacy, modern engineering practices, and responsible innovation. Drive a culture of curiosity, learning, inclusion, and continuous improvement. Budgeting & Partner Management

Manage budgets, forecasts, and vendor contracts, including AI platforms, tooling, and service providers. Evaluate build vs. buy decisions for AI and digital capabilities. Characteristics We Seek

Curious about technology and AI: Actively explores how emerging technologies and AI can improve patient, clinician, and associate experiences. Continuous learner: Invests in ongoing growth for self and teams, especially in rapidly evolving AI and engineering domains. Change-ready leader: Navigates ambiguity, drives transformation, and leads teams through technological and organizational change. Feedback-driven: Embeds feedback loops in software, AI systems, and leadership practices. Self-aware and situationally aware: Balances confidence with humility and sound judgment. Positive and resilient: Approaches complex healthcare challenges with optimism and purpose. Collaborative: Values teamwork, mentorship, and cross-functional partnership. Values-driven: Lives Ascension’s core values of service of the poor, reverence, integrity, wisdom, creativity, and dedication.

Requirements Education:

High School diploma equivalency with 5 years of applicable cumulative job specific experience required, with 2 of those years being in leadership/management OR Associate's degree/Bachelor's degree with 3 years of applicable cumulative job specific experience required, with 2 of those years being in leadership/management.

Additional Preferences Expertise in architecting and delivering scalable, highly available, consumer-facing digital platforms. Strong experience with AI systems, including model integration, inference services, vector databases, embeddings, and retrieval-augmented generation (RAG). Hands-on knowledge of AI and data platforms (e.g., GCP Vertex AI, Azure AI, OpenAI ecosystem, open-source ML frameworks). Experience with modern search, integration, microservices, and data technologies (GraphQL/REST, Spring Boot, Cloud SQL, NoSQL). Experience with observability platforms (e.g., Dynatrace, Datadog) and AI/model monitoring. Cloud-native experience in GCP and/or Azure, including healthcare-specific services. Experience with experimentation, feature flags, and A/B testing. Strong understanding of healthcare interoperability standards (FHIR, HL7) and regulations (HIPAA, Cures Act), especially as applied to AI. Deep computer science fundamentals and modern software engineering best practices across the full SDLC. Demonstrated experience leading large engineering organizations and complex transformations. 15+ years of software engineering experience, with 5+ years in senior engineering leadership roles. Excellent written and verbal communication skills. #GMDx

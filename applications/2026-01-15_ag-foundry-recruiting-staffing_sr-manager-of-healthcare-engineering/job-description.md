Job Description

About the job Our client is seeking a Senior Manager of Healthcare Engineering to lead and execute engineering initiatives across a growing healthcare product portfolio. This is a hands-on leadership role for a technically strong manager who enjoys staying close to architecture, system design, and delivery while leading and developing engineering teams.

This role partners closely with Product and Operations to translate business and product priorities into scalable, secure, and high-performing software systems. The Senior Manager will be accountable for both execution and technical direction, with direct involvement in key engineering decisions.

Responsibilities:

Lead day-to-day engineering execution while contributing to longer-term technical direction and platform evolution. Translate product and business priorities into actionable engineering plans, architectural decisions, and delivery roadmaps. Serve as a hands-on technical leader, staying close to system design, code reviews, and complex technical decisions. Guide development of cloud-native, data-driven systems with a strong emphasis on Python-based backend services. Drive improvements in APIs, data platforms, and interoperability aligned with healthcare standards such as HIPAA, HL7, and FHIR. Build, mentor, and manage high-performing engineering teams with clear expectations around quality, ownership, and accountability. Coach senior engineers and team leads while maintaining strong technical standards. Champion continuous improvement in architecture, technical debt reduction, automation, and DevOps practices. Ensure reliable, on-time delivery across multiple products and initiatives. Establish and monitor engineering KPIs related to delivery velocity, quality, and system reliability. Partner with Product, Security, and Operations to ensure performance, scalability, and regulatory compliance. Act as an escalation point for complex technical issues and delivery risks. Support infrastructure modernization, CI/CD enhancements, and cloud reliability initiatives. Manage team capacity, external vendors, and engineering budgets within defined constraints. Qualifications:

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field; advanced degree preferred. 10+ years of software engineering experience, including several years in engineering leadership or management roles. Experience in healthcare technology or other highly regulated environments strongly preferred. Strong background building and maintaining Python-based backend systems, APIs, and cloud-native services. Experience working in AWS-based environments and modern DevOps toolchains. Proven ability to balance hands-on technical work with people leadership responsibilities. Solid understanding of distributed systems, data-driven architectures, and system scalability. Strong communication skills with the ability to collaborate across technical and non-technical stakeholders. Track record of leading teams through growth, change, and increasing technical complexity. Experience managing budgets, vendors, or external partners is a plus.

## About Upstart

Upstart is the leading AI lending marketplace, connecting millions of consumers to 100+ banks and credit unions. Platform runs 1M+ predictions per borrower using 1,800+ signals. 90%+ of loans fully automated. Products include personal loans, auto retail/refinance, HELOCs, and small-dollar relief loans. Public company (NASDAQ: UPST), ~1,821 employees, 3.5 year median tenure. Founded 2012, based in San Mateo, CA.

## The Team

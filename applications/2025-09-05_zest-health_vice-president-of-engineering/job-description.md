Job Description
About the job

About Us:

Zest Health is redefining the treatment of chronic dermatologic conditions through our tech-enabled, virtual model. We focus on high-touch, whole-person dermatology care, starting with patients managing psoriasis and eczema. Our mission is to transform the approach to treatment, making care more accessible, effective, and affordable. Join us as we set the new gold standard for value-based specialty care.

Job Summary:

Zest Health is seeking a VP of Engineering to join our growing team. In this role, you will own the technological systems that allow us to deliver high-quality, affordable dermatology care at scale – and lead the team that supports that function. As our company continues on our rapid and exciting growth trajectory, you will ensure that our rise continues to be underpinned by technological excellence.

Core Responsibilities:

Build and maintain a world-class healthcare platform:

Evaluate, approve, and prioritize software projects and resources according to importance to the business and ROI, clearly communicating the risks and opportunities associated with key decisions
Strategically develop and tactically deploy software development plans that improve productivity, security, quality, and platform performance
Assess the organization’s current and future growth requirements to develop resource and technical workplans that meet its evolving needs
Partner cross-functionally on best technology selection and implementation, ensuring delivery of performance and cost objectives
Own the technology vendor stack and relationships with key business partners such as Amazon Web Service

Drive engineering team excellence:

Lead, support, train, and develop the Engineering staff
Ensure on-time and on-budget delivery of engineering initiatives
Continuously improve and refine our engineering and technical processes
Foster a culture of high performance, accountability, and continuous improvement
Cultivate a cross-functional IT organization in collaboration with engineering

Ensure systems are secure and compliant

Coordinate and manage various audits of our tech stack (more detail below) to ensure continuous HIPAA and SOC 2 compliance
Explore and implement operational and technology solutions that support the growth agenda and enable operational excellence for all Zest employees

Our Tech Stack:

Zest Dermatology leverages various open source and production-grade technologies:

Amazon Web Services with IaC (Terraform) configuration and a best-practice, security hardened, multi-account architecture
Docker-based stateless containerized workloads run production using AWS Fargate
Django and Postgres for a singular data model shared across all business applications
A modern frontend using HTMX, Alpine, and Web Components
Automated business-side reporting built on Streamlit
Use of Nix and NixOS for consistently reproducible development environments (exclusively Mac based) as well as reproducible build artifacts (docker images, AMIs) and CI/CD pipelines
Automated CI/CD infrastructure using self-hosted GitHub Actions runners

Qualifications:

10+ years of experience in software engineering, with experience in AWS and other pieces of our tech stack
3+ years of experience managing and leading teams of other engineers
Experience working in a regulated industry required, with specific healthcare experience a huge plus
Experience working at an early-stage startup (Series A through C) is a huge plus
Based in the United States

Why join the Zest Team?

Do meaningful work alongside dynamic, mission-driven teammates
Opportunity to join a high-growth start-up that is revolutionizing care delivery for dermatology patients
Competitive compensation and equity package
Employer-sponsored medical, dental, and vision plans

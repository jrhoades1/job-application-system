Job Description

About the job Overview

Our client is seeking a Chief Technology Officer (CTO) to define and lead the organization’s long-term technology vision, architecture, and execution strategy in alignment with business objectives. This executive role is responsible for driving scalable, secure, and innovative technology solutions while partnering closely with the CEO and executive leadership team to enable growth, product differentiation, and operational excellence.

Key Responsibilities

Define and own the enterprise technology strategy, roadmap, and architectural direction aligned with business and product goals. Lead all technology functions, including software engineering, platform architecture, infrastructure, DevOps, security, and IT (as applicable). Partner with the CEO, Product, and executive leadership to translate business strategy into scalable and reliable technical solutions. Oversee the design, development, deployment, and maintenance of customer-facing and internal technology platforms. Establish engineering standards, development best practices, and governance to ensure quality, performance, and scalability. Ensure strong cybersecurity, data privacy, and risk management practices across all technology systems. Drive technology innovation, platform modernization, and adoption of emerging tools to improve speed, reliability, and competitive advantage. Manage technology budgets, resource planning, and vendor relationships to optimize cost and delivery outcomes. Build, mentor, and scale a high-performing engineering and technology leadership team. Identify and mitigate technical debt and operational risks while supporting rapid business growth.

Operating Context

Reports directly to the Chief Executive Officer and serves as a core member of the executive leadership team. Works closely with Product, Data, Operations, and Finance leaders to prioritize initiatives and manage trade-offs. Oversees multi-disciplinary technology teams supporting company-wide platforms and products. Operates in a fast-paced, high-growth environment requiring strong execution, strategic thinking, and cross-functional influence.

Success Metrics

Scalability, reliability, and performance of technology platforms and infrastructure. On-time, high-quality delivery of product and technology initiatives aligned with business priorities. Security posture, uptime, and effectiveness of risk mitigation efforts. Engineering productivity, quality, and team engagement. Executive and stakeholder confidence in technology strategy and execution. Ability to support growth without disproportionate increases in cost or complexity.

Required Qualifications

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related technical field. 12+ years of progressive experience in software engineering or technology leadership roles. Proven experience leading large-scale engineering teams and complex technology platforms. Strong background in modern software architectures, cloud infrastructure, and development methodologies. Demonstrated ability to align technology strategy with business outcomes at the executive level. Exceptional leadership, communication, and decision-making skills.

Preferred Qualifications

Master’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or Business (MBA). Prior experience as a CTO, VP of Engineering, or equivalent senior technology leader. Experience scaling technology organizations in high-growth, startup, or SaaS environments. Deep expertise in cloud platforms, DevOps, security, and data-driven systems. Track record of leading digital transformation or platform modernization initiatives.

This position is posted by Jobgether on behalf of a partner company. We are currently looking for a Full Stack Engineering Manager - REMOTE. In this role, you will have a significant impact as you lead a team of talented engineers focused on building cloud-native systems that serve a diverse array of users. By bridging technical design with practical implementation, you will ensure that projects are completed efficiently while upholding high standards of quality. Your expertise will also facilitate seamless collaboration across remote and local teams, empowering the next generation of modern solutions for content creation. We are eager to find someone who not only has a strong technical background but is also passionate about enhancing user experiences.
Accountabilities
Lead and manage a team to build well-architected web and mobile apps.
Guide team through research, design, implementation, and delivery.
Influence technical direction for cross-functional teams.
Write code samples and prototypes to support implementation.
Promote modern technical standards and best practices.
Encourage quality code and effective communication within the team.
Maintain communication with local and remote partners on project statuses.
Support team members' performance with feedback and development goals.
Hire and train new engineering talent.
Collaborate with designers and business leaders to enhance user experiences.
Engage with customers to refine workflows and address challenges.
Requirements
5+ years of experience in web application development.
3+ years of experience managing technical teams.
BA/BS in Computer Science or equivalent work experience.
Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and modern frameworks (Vue, React).
Experience with backend services using Java, Python, or Node.
Familiarity with AI tools for improving code quality.
Strong track record in designing and developing scalable applications.
Knowledge of web development best practices.
Bonus: Experience with scalable content management systems.
Familiarity with testing frameworks and static code analysis tools.
Experience managing AWS deployments and services.
Excellent communication skills and ability to work cooperatively.
Passion for building innovative productivity solutions.
Experience with agile software development methodologies.
Benefits
Flexible work schedule with the option to work from anywhere.
Competitive salary and performance-based bonuses.
Opportunities for professional development and career growth.
Health and wellness programs to support employee well-being.
Collaborative and inclusive company culture.
Access to cutting-edge tools and technologies.
Work with a talented and passionate team.

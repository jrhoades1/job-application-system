Job Description
About the job

About ScalePost.ai

ScalePost.ai is the infrastructure layer connecting LLMs and AI agents with the world’s top content owners. We facilitate licensing and monetization partnerships between AI companies and over 100 leading publishers—including Time, Entrepreneur, and The Texas Tribune—across text, audio, video, and image formats. Our relationships with companies like Perplexity.ai and our expanding suite of analytics and bot management tools make it seamless for AI systems to access high-quality content at scale.

We are backed by top-tier investors and advisors from Quiet Capital, E14 Fund, Tenacity Ventures, Blank Ventures and the Ex-CEO of Fast Company & Inc. Magazine, ex. Chief Data Officer of Hulu and co-founders of transformative AI technologies like Siri.

About the Role

We’re looking for a Head of Engineering who will play a critical role in building and scaling our platform. You’ll be responsible for laying the technical foundation of the company, leading engineering efforts from 0 to 1, and driving our infrastructure and data strategy. This is a rare opportunity for a mission-driven builder to join at the ground floor and shape both the technology and culture of a fast-moving company.

The ideal candidate

8+ years of engineering experience, ideally with a background in startups or high-growth environments.
Has previously built and scaled a product from scratch.
Strong skills in infrastructure, cloud technologies (AWS, GCP, or Azure), APIs, and deployment pipelines.
Expertise in agile development and ability to build a strong engineering culture.
Exceptional communication skills.
Embraces a can-do attitude, gets things done quickly, and enjoys delivering impact.
Strong experience in rapid deployments, and optimizing engineering workflows.
Capable of hiring, mentoring, and scaling a high-performing engineering team.
We are looking for someone who values transparency, accountability, and trust.
Excited about the mission of reshaping how content and data are consumed by AI through technology.

What You'll Do

Build the foundation of the ScalePost.ai platform, focusing on scalability, security, and fast deployments.
Lead architecture decisions around data infrastructure, APIs, and integrations with media and AI platforms.
Manage the end-to-end product lifecycle from concept, design, deployment, and iteration.
Hire and mentor top-tier engineers who align with our mission and culture.
Collaborate closely with the co-founders to define technical strategy and roadmap.
Ensure seamless collaboration between engineering, product, and operations teams.
Continuously improve deployment processes to ensure fast, efficient rollouts.
Be hands-on with code and architecture, but also effective in managing and scaling teams as the company grows.

Job Description

Datavant is a data platform company and the world’s leader in health data exchange. Our vision is that every healthcare decision is powered by the right data, at the right time, in the right format.

Our platform is powered by the largest, most diverse health data network in the U.S., enabling data to be secure, accessible and usable to inform better health decisions. Datavant is trusted by the world’s leading life sciences companies, government agencies, and those who deliver and pay for care. 

By joining Datavant today, you’re stepping onto a high-performing, values-driven team. Together, we’re rising to the challenge of tackling some of healthcare’s most complex problems with technology-forward solutions. Datavanters bring a diversity of professional, educational and life experiences to realize our bold vision for healthcare.

As a Director of Engineering, you will be an engineering leader who shapes what we do and will set the tone for how we do it. You will be part of the Connections Platform team, which includes dedicated pods building EHR integrations, building connections products for provider, payer and life sciences products. This specific role will focus on building connectivity with affiliates as per the data needs of payer, life sciences, attorneys and insurance organizations. You will be working closely with various product teams across the growing Datavant customer base to enhance data exchange between a wide range of data providers. 

Specific requirements: 

Been managing 2+ teams and managing managers 
Can taken on more scope as their team(s) grow 
Strategic product minded leader 
Great cross functional stakeholder management skills 
Handling ambiguity and trade offs, a big part of the job is being able to identify requirements and finding the right solution to use to solve the problem. You might have to dive into other teams' codebases to collaborate with them on building a solution in their ownership area. 
Having interoperability and data access experience is a plus

 

In this role you will:

Build, grow, and lead a world-class team of engineers who build the critical parts of our product platforms
Create the technical vision for Datavant’s suite of data connectivity solutions.
Partner with product and design to ship value to customers
Be a critical leader of the provider business , delivering rapid revenue and customer growth by directing how customer use cases, capabilities, positioning, and more come together to create a winning product.
Be the champion & driver of product innovation processes & initiatives.
Be hands-on by leading high impact efforts.
Be a strong communicator , coach, and mentor.

What You Bring to the Table:

You have 15+ years of engineering experience including multiple years in leadership, managing managers, building scrappy teams from scratch and building large enterprise software.
You are passionate about building a business that transforms the healthcare industry
You have a hunger to win
You can both “lead” and “do”
You are ambitious, scrappy, tenacious, and you get things done
You are comfortable making hard decisions and move quickly
You are noted as one of the best problem solvers in your organization
You have experience managing a highly functioning team of engineering teams
You have experience in healthcare or other highly regulated field like financial services
You are highly organized and reliable; you bring structure to complexity

About the Opportunity

We are partnering with a growth‑stage B2B software company that builds and distributes products on the Salesforce Platform. The company is seeking a Vice President of Engineering to lead engineering execution, guide technical strategy, and scale a high‑performing team in a platform‑constrained environment.

This role is designed for an engineering leader who has operated successfully as a Salesforce ISV, understands the realities of building within the Salesforce ecosystem, and has recently helped incorporate Salesforce Platform AI capabilities into a production product.

The Role

As VP of Engineering, you will be responsible for how engineering operates, how the platform evolves, and how teams scale. You’ll work closely with Product and go‑to‑market leadership to ensure the organization delivers high‑quality, secure, and reliable software while navigating the architectural, security, and packaging requirements of the Salesforce platform.

You’ll inherit a small, primarily remote engineering team and will be expected to both set direction and stay close to the details, stepping in when needed to unblock teams or guide critical technical decisions.

Key Responsibilities

Serve as a senior member of the leadership team, representing engineering priorities, risks, and tradeoffs Own engineering delivery across multiple initiatives, balancing roadmap execution with architectural integrity and long‑term maintainability Partner closely with Product leadership to translate business goals into technical direction, delivery plans, and staffing needs Establish and continuously improve engineering operating rhythms, including planning, sprint cycles, and release management Set technical standards and guardrails for a Salesforce‑native architecture, including performance, scalability, extensibility, and upgrade paths Lead development and evolution of applications built and distributed as a Salesforce ISV Drive adoption of Salesforce Platform AI capabilities (e.g., platform‑native AI, copilots, predictive features, or embedded automation) within customer‑facing functionality Promote responsible use of AI‑assisted development practices to improve developer productivity, test coverage, and documentation Build, coach, and retain a high‑performing engineering organization through hiring, career development, and leadership mentoring Improve engineering effectiveness across delivery reliability, code quality, system health, and team sustainability Ensure compliance with Salesforce security, data privacy, and ISV review requirements Roll up your sleeves when necessary to resolve complex issues or support high‑impact releases

What We’re Looking For

Required Qualifications

Proven experience leading engineering execution and technical strategy in a B2B SaaS environment Direct experience working at a Salesforce ISV, including packaging, distribution, security review, and managed releases Recent hands‑on experience incorporating Salesforce Platform AI capabilities into production product features Track record of building, scaling, and developing engineering teams in mid‑sized organizations (roughly 25–200 employees) Strong technical background designing and scaling customer‑facing applications within Salesforce platform constraints Experience guiding frontend architecture using technologies such as JavaScript, TypeScript, and component‑based UI frameworks Strong technical and organizational judgment in ambiguous environments Clear, effective communicator who can align engineering execution with product and business strategy

Preferred Qualifications

Experience modernizing or extending Salesforce‑native architectures while maintaining compliance and upgradeability Familiarity with AI‑driven product discovery and feasibility evaluation in a Salesforce context Experience establishing engineering metrics beyond velocity (predictability, quality, system health) Leadership style rooted in accountability, transparency, and continuous improvement

Team & Environment

Lean, distributed engineering team (currently under 10 contributors) Mix of platform, frontend, and QA expertise Predominantly remote with selective in‑person collaboration Emphasis on ownership, pragmatism, and sustainable delivery

Compensation & Benefits

Competitive base salary commensurate with experience, plus bonus and equity Comprehensive medical, dental, and vision insurance Flexible paid time off Generous paid family and medical leave Retirement plan with company match Additional benefits and stipends supporting work‑life balance

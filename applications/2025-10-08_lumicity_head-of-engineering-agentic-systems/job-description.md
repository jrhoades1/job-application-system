🚀 Head of Engineering – Agentic AI (Stealth Growth Opportunity)

Some opportunities feel like just another job. Others give you a genuine chance to shape the future of healthcare with cutting-edge AI. This is one of those roles.

I’m working with a hypergrowth AI startup (fresh off a major funding round, backed by top-tier investors) that’s building an entire ecosystem of “AI employees.” They’re already trusted by hundreds of healthcare providers, and now they’re looking for a Head of Engineering to scale their technical foundations.

The right person will:

✅ Have founder/early-stage DNA: Someone who’s been the first engineering hire, seed-stage CTO, or builder who thrives in ambiguity and can create orgs/processes from scratch.

✅ Be a hands-on coder/architect with real-world deployment of agentic AI (LLMs, RAG, LangChain, LangGraph, Anthropic, VertexAI). This isn’t just research or prototyping; it’s production-scale systems.

✅ Know how to scale with discipline: Building engineering teams (10 → 50+), driving measurable improvements (velocity, cost, uptime), and ensuring KPIs are tracked and delivered.

✅ Bring compliance and rigor: Experience in HIPAA, GDPR, SOC-2, and the systems/processes needed to ship securely in healthcare and SaaS.

✅ Bonus: Visible open-source/community leadership , whether it’s GitHub commits, framework contributions, or thought leadership.

If you’re looking for a role where you’ll still be close to the code and build an org that scales globally, this is a chance to lead from the front.

📩 DM me if this feels like your kind of challenge.

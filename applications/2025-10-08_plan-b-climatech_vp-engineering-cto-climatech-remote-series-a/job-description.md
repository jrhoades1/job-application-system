Job Description
About the job

I am working with a high growth Series A funded ClimaTech business who are looking for their first VP Engineering/ CTO. We are looking for someone who has a background in Big Tech but has also scaled start up's recently, ideally through multiple rounds of funding.

You will have a solid engineering background but _ must _ have direct, hands on experience with the following:

Ingestion: Ability to architect multi-source data pipelines (APIs, PDFs, DOCX, spreadsheets) with complex parsing steps; familiarity with tools such as Unstructured, Markitdown, LlamaParse.
Retrieval and Reranking: Fluency in building retrieval strategies (including query planning, expansion, and rewriting) on top of dense, sparse and hybrid embeddings; familiarity with standard off-the-shelf vector DBs
Evals: Experience building and maintaining strong evaluation suites for RAG pipelines; familiarity with tools such as DeepEval, Ragas.
Prompt Ops: Ability to reason about and engineer LLM contexts to maximize eval metrics while maintaining a balance between latency and pipeline reliability; familiarity with standard prompt engineering strategies such as pruning, compression, offloading to memory
Model Ops: Experience with LLM routing (optimizing quality output per token-$), retry/fallback strategies, semantic caching; familiarity with LiteLLM, OpenRouter
Agentic Systems: Experience building long-running tool-use agents with retry/ compensation logic; familiarity with AutoGen, LangGraph, Agno, AgentOps
Systems Engineering: Experience optimizing end-to-end AI pipelines (ingestion ➜ retrieval ➜ LLM ➜ post-processing) for latency/throughput/cost budgets; experience with LLM observability tooling.

If you have hands on experience with the above and the role sounds interesting please share your resume ASAP.

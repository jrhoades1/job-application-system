Job Description

Keeper Security is hiring a world-class Director of Engineering to join the Office of the CTO and help shape the future of our engineering organization. This is a unique role for an exceptional technologist who still writes code, understands systems end-to-end, and thrives on solving high-stakes challenges across infrastructure, application, and architecture. This is a 100% remote position with an opportunity to work a hybrid schedule for candidates based in the El Dorado Hills, CA area.

Keeper’s cybersecurity software is trusted by millions of people and thousands of organizations, globally. Join one of the fastest-growing cybersecurity companies and be responsible for expanding and architecting Keeper's backend projects in the AWS cloud with the latest technology and tools!

About Keeper

Keeper Security is transforming cybersecurity for organizations around the world with next-generation privileged access management. Keeper’s zero-trust and zero-knowledge cybersecurity solutions are FedRAMP and StateRAMP Authorized, FIPS 140-2 validated, as well as SOC 2 and ISO 27001 certified. Keeper deploys in minutes, not months, and seamlessly integrates with any tech stack to prevent breaches, reduce help desk costs and ensure compliance. Trusted by thousands of organizations to protect every user on every device, Keeper is the industry leader for best-in-class password management, secrets management, privileged access, secure remote access and encrypted messaging. Learn more atKeeperSecurity.com.

About the Role

As Director of Engineering in the Office of the CTO, you’ll serve as a senior engineering partner to the CTO—helping to accelerate technical initiatives, shape architecture, solve high-priority challenges, and deliver mission-critical solutions. This is not a traditional people-management role. Instead, you’ll lead by example through code, architecture, and strategic execution. You'll work cross-functionally across teams and domains, drive technical alignment, and step in wherever technical depth, velocity, or clarity is needed most.

Responsibilities

Serve as a hands-on engineering leader embedded in the Office of the CTO, supporting high-impact technical initiatives across the company

Design, architect, and write high-performance, scalable code across systems—from backend infrastructure to security layers and integration workflows

Build and deliver technical proofs of concept, prototypes, and reference implementations for strategic projects

Drive architectural reviews and influence design decisions across engineering domains

Identify, triage, and lead solutions for bottlenecks in reliability, performance, observability, and security

Lead strategic R&D workstreams including platform modernization, automation tooling, DevSecOps pipelines, and secure development practices

Serve as a technical representative in executive meetings, engineering leadership syncs, and board briefings

Evaluate and recommend technologies, vendors, or tools to improve platform scalability, developer velocity, or system security

Collaborate with engineering teams to ensure alignment on long-term technical strategy, roadmap planning, and cross-functional deliverables

Provide mentorship and guidance to technical leads and senior engineers across the organization

Participate in post-incident reviews, architectural assessments, and engineering hiring efforts

Requirements

10+ years of professional software engineering experience with expertise in building secure, scalable, and resilient systems

Deep hands-on coding ability across multiple languages (e.g., Java, C++, Python, React, or similar) and environments (cloud-native, backend services, distributed systems)

Strong architectural experience with system design, API strategy, infrastructure, and security

Proven track record of technical leadership in complex, high-scale environments (e.g., infrastructure, security, platform, dev tools)

Strong experience with AWS cloud services and modern CI/CD workflows

Familiarity with secrets management, privileged access management, or cybersecurity architecture is highly preferred

Bachelor's or Master’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related technical field—or equivalent hands-on experience

Outstanding communication and leadership skills with the ability to influence and align teams at all levels of the organization

Comfortable operating in a fast-paced, evolving environment with a high degree of autonomy and responsibility

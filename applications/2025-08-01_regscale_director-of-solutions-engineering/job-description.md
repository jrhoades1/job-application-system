Job Description

RegScale is a continuous controls monitoring (CCM) platform purpose-built to deliver fast and efficient GRC outcomes. We help organizations break out of the slow and expensive realities that plague legacy GRC tools by bridging security, risk, and compliance through controls lifecycle management. By leveraging CCM, organizations experience massive process improvements like 90% faster certification times, and 60% less audit prep time. Today’s expansive security and compliance requirements can only be met with a modern, CCM based approach, and RegScale is the leader in that space.

Position:

Are you ready to accelerate your career by joining a fast-growing, mission-driven startup that’s transforming the compliance industry? RegScale is seeking a Director of Solutions Engineering to lead and scale a high-performing Solutions Engineering team. You will play a pivotal role in driving pre-sales success, customer value realization, and technical strategy across our go-to-market efforts.

In this leadership role, you’ll lead technical engagement across the enterprise sales cycle—from discovery and solution architecture to proof of concept and customer onboarding. You will also help shape our product direction by translating market feedback into strategic requirements and work closely with the Product team to build what customers want. If you thrive in fast-paced environments, enjoy mentoring technical talent, and are passionate about solving meaningful customer problems, we’d love to meet you.

Position must be a US Citizen and is subject to a background check and unannounced drug testing requirements. We know how valuable your creativity and technical skills are to our company’s success, and we offer an exceptional salary and benefits package commensurate with the responsibilities of the position. If you’re ready to lead our Solution Engineering team to deliver meaningful outcomes for our customers, then this is the place for you!

Key Responsibilities:

Leadership & Strategy

Customer-Facing Execution

Operational Excellence

Required Skills:

Proven experience building, mentoring, and scaling high-performing Sales Engineering teams in fast-paced environments.
Cybersecurity expertise in deploying, operating, or selling cybersecurity products
Outstanding communication skills with the ability to convey complex ideas to both technical and non-technical audiences.
Experience delivering technical sales presentations, POCs, and integration demos for B2B enterprise customers.

Preferred Skills:

Deep knowledge of compliance frameworks such as NIST SP 800-53, FedRamp, RMF, ISO 27001, PCI-DSS, CMMC , etc.
Hands-on technical expertise in scripting (Python, Bash, etc.), data integration, APIs, and cloud platforms.

Education/Training, Qualifications, and Certification:

Bachelor's degree in a technical discipline
10+ years of experience in Enterprise Software/SaaS, including 5+ years in Sales Engineering and 3+ years in leadership roles.

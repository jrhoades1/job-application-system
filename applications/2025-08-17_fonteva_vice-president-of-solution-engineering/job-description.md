Job Description

Vice President of Solution Engineering
Location: Remote US Based: Austin, TX Preferred
Reports to: Chief Revenue Officer

Are you a strategic thinker with deep technical expertise and a passion for building high-performing teams? Join us as Vice President of Solution Engineering and take the lead in shaping how we deliver value to customers and scale our solutions globally. If you thrive in dynamic, fast-growing environments and want to be part of a company where your ideas and leadership will have real impact, we want to hear from you.

What You’ll Do
In this role, you will take full ownership of our global Solution Engineering strategy and execution. You will:

Grow and lead a high-performing global team of solution engineers supporting sales, partnerships, and customer success

Shape the product roadmap by capturing and prioritizing customer and market insights from the field

Design scalable go-to-market strategies in collaboration with Sales, Marketing, and Product

Evaluate and onboard new technology and GTM partners to enhance our offering and expand our market presence

Identify AI integration opportunities that can improve internal processes, enhance product capabilities, or open new revenue streams

Support international expansion by defining technical strategies and allocating resources for market entry, compliance, and local support

Act as a senior technical advisor in complex client situations to support retention and account growth

What You Bring
You’ll succeed in this role if you have:

10+ years of experience in solution engineering, pre-sales, or technical sales leadership

A strong track record of managing and scaling global technical teams

Deep understanding of enterprise B2B SaaS, cloud platforms, and AI technologies

Proven ability to collaborate across departments including Sales, Product, and Marketing

Experience leading international go-to-market efforts and localization strategies

Excellent communication, strategic planning, and analytical skills

Bonus Points For

Experience in high-growth startup or scale-up environments

Familiarity with partnership ecosystems and enterprise AI/ML solutions

A technical degree or MBA

What You’ll Get
This is a unique opportunity to:

Play a pivotal role in the company’s global growth strategy

Lead a team that sits at the intersection of technology, sales, and customer success

Influence product development based on real-world market insights

Work remotely with a flexible, global team and forward-thinking leadership

We're looking for someone who’s not only ready to take on a senior leadership role but excited to shape how technology meets business needs in a high-impact, global organization. If that sounds like you, apply now.

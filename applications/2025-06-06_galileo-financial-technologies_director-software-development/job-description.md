The Role:

We are seeking an experienced Director of Engineering to lead the technical strategy, development and execution of both our Digital Banking Framework and Digital Banking Platform. This is a high-impact leadership role responsible for building the foundational infrastructure that powers our hosted customer-facing experiences.

This leader will drive the design and delivery of platform capabilities that support scalable, performant, and secure digital products. You’ll manage multiple teams, align cross-functional stakeholders, and ensure the frameworks and services your org delivers accelerate—not constrain—product development across the company.

What You’ll Do:

Own the technical vision and delivery execution

Collaborate closely with product, design, and other engineering leaders to align platform work with business needs

Ensure the platform supports rapid product development across dozens of different customer programs while maintaining quality, compliance, and operational excellence

Attract, retain, and develop top-tier engineering talent

Foster a culture of technical excellence, ownership, and customer-centric thinking

Manage multiple remote engineering teams across platform and framework domains

Ensure reliable, secure, and scalable engineering practices

Measure and improve time-to-market metrics

 

What You’ll Need:

Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related technical field or equivalent experience.
10+ years in software engineering, including 5+ years of proven experience in leadership roles, preferably within mobile and web domain, including backend.
Deep expertise in designing and managing cloud-based solutions with a strong focus on resiliency .
Strong understanding of agile development principles and tools.
Excellent leadership and communication skills, with the ability to influence and collaborate effectively across teams.
Experience with AWS (or other public cloud infra) and modern mobile stacks
Experience working in a regulated industry, such as finance, is a plus.
Bi-Lingual Spanish/English is required.

POSITION SUMMARY:

The Director- Software Engineering leads platform and data engineering functions that power Cyroport Systems. The role defines and executes the technology, data, and AI strategy that supports growth, compliance, and innovation. This leader owns delivery of key digital platforms, ensuring architectures are scalable, secure, and insight driven. This role fosters a higher performance culture that combines engineering excellence with automation and intelligent systems.

PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES (include but are not limited to):

• Define and execute multi quarter technology, platform, and data roadmaps aligned with business objectives

• Lead architecture, design, and delivery of enterprise platforms supporting cold chain orchestration, analytics and automation

• Establish data and AI infrastructure including data lakes, pipelines, and governance frameworks

• Integrate predictive analytics and AI models into products and operations

• Evaluate emerging AI and platform technologies for scalability, cost efficiency, and regulatory alignment

• Partner with Product, Finance, and Executive Leadership to prioritize investments and track measurable results

• Build, mentor, and retain senior engineering talent; promote diversity, inclusion and accountability

• Implement scaled Agile delivery processes and maintain performance dashboards for velocity, reliability, and quality

• Govern technology budgets and resource allocation across projects and platforms

• Partner with Security, Compliance, and Legal to ensure all solutions meet regulatory standards such as HIPAA, GxP, and SOC 2

• Direct research and development for AI, data, and IoT initiatives; transition validated pilots into production platforms

• Serve as the primary technology liaison to executive stakeholders, communicating progress, risk, and ROI with clarity

• Converts business strategy into platform and data execution plans that scale

• Builds a resilient engineering organization that delivers reliable and compliant technology

• Ensures architectural coherence, measurable performance, and sustained innovation

• Serves as the executive partner guiding long-term platform growth and intelligent systems adoption

QUALIFICATIONS AND EDUCATION REQUIREMENTS:

• Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related technical discipline (required); Master’s degree (preferred)

• 12+ years of professional software engineering experience, including at least 5 years in a leadership role overseeing multiple engineering teams (e.g., Senior Engineering Manager, Director- Engineering)

• Demonstrated success defining and delivering large-scale technology initiatives in a regulated environment (e.g., life sciences, logistics), with accountability for P&L or equivalent budget ownership.

• Proven track record building and scaling high-performing engineering organizations, recruiting senior talent, and developing leaders.

About Salesforce

We are on a mission to transform the developer experience within the Hyperforce program by building cutting-edge platforms and tooling through our Forward Engineering Team. Role Overview:
 

As the Senior Director of Engineering of the Forward Engineering Team, you will be a pivotal leader in shaping and executing the strategic vision for the developer experience across multiple cloud providers. You will lead a small, focused senior engineering team, fostering a culture of innovation, excellence, and empowerment. This role requires a blend of deep technical expertise, strategic thinking, and exceptional leadership to guide the team in onboarding external developers into the Hyperforce development process and partnering to deliver key initiatives.
 

Location:

* 10+ years of experience in software engineering, DevOps, or a related field, with a proven track record of technical leadership.
* 5+ years of experience in a senior management role, leading and mentoring high performing engineering teams.
* Significant experience with cloud platforms such as AWS, GCP, or Azure.
* Demonstrated experience in driving the strategy and execution for a technical product or platform.
* Familiarity with mergers and acquisitions
* Excellent communication, interpersonal, and leadership skills, with the ability to inspire and motivate a team.
* Proven ability to work effectively in a dynamic environment and manage competing priorities.
* A strategic mindset with a passion for improving the developer experience and a drive to solve complex technical challenges.
* Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience).

What Success in this role looks like: 

* Establish a clear vision for onboarding new external developers into Salesforce as part of potential M&A activity 
* Drive measurable reduction across new developer friction points on Hyperforce around productivity, developer satisfaction, and platform adoption.
* Build successful internal partnerships to enable cultural shifts where engineers prefer paved paths over custom tooling because the experience is intuitive, powerful, and fast.
* Lead, mentor, and grow a high-performing engineering team. Foster a collaborative and inclusive environment that encourages innovation, professional growth, and a strong sense of ownership.
* Drive the successful execution of complex technical projects, ensuring timely delivery of high-quality tooling and services. 
* Implement and refine agile development processes to optimize team productivity and responsiveness.

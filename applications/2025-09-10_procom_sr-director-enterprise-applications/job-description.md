Job Description

Sr Director Enterprise Applications:
On behalf of our healthcare client, Procom is searching for a Sr Director Enterprise Applications for a permanent role. This position is a remote position based in Chicago, Illinois.

Sr Director Enterprise Applications - Job Description:
The Senior Director, Enterprise Systems will lead the vision, strategy, and execution of the enterprise applications portfolio. This executive leader will oversee the delivery and optimization of critical systems that power clinical, business, and research operations, ensuring alignment with the mission to improve health through excellence in patient care, education, research, and community partnerships.

Sr Director Enterprise Applications - Responsibilities:

Provide strategic leadership for enterprise applications, including ERP (Workday), CRM (ServiceNow), and other core technologies.
Partner with senior leadership to shape IT strategy, roadmaps, and investments.
Lead a team of talented professionals, fostering a culture of collaboration, growth, and accountability.
Manage a multimillion-dollar portfolio, ensuring operational excellence, vendor alignment, and delivery of transformative solutions.
Develop and direct the Enterprise Systems application portfolio function within D&IS.
Collaborate with senior D&IS leadership to facilitate development of a master IT Strategic plan.
Oversee development of standards and processes to manage the Enterprise Systems software application portfolio.
Facilitate process improvement and integration of strategic priorities across the Enterprise Systems software application portfolio.
Promote effective, open communication and develop collaborative working relationships with all levels of staff.

Sr Director Enterprise Applications - Mandatory Skills:

Bachelor’s degree required.
Ten to fifteen years of previous experience in IS management within a dynamic and complex health system.
Demonstrated executive leadership skills with a mature sense of priorities.
ERP (Workday) implementation and optimization experience is required.
Experience in application management and driving best practices.
Proven ability to design and drive change.
Strong analytical and organizational skills.

Sr Director Enterprise Applications – Nice-to-Have Skills:

CRM (ServiceNow) implementation and optimization experience preferred.
Experience with repeatable application delivery and support processes.
Ability to identify and resolve highly complex ethical, strategic, and healthcare operational problems.
Demonstrated effectiveness as a thought leader and business partner.
Proficient level of knowledge of applicable data privacy practices and laws.

Sr Director Enterprise Applications – Assignment Length:
This is a permanent position.

ASAP.

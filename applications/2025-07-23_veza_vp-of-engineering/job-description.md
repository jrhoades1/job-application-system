Job Description

Veza is leading the next wave of cybersecurity innovation as the first and only platform built on the power of authorization. Under the leadership of CEO and Co-Founder Tarun Thakur, Veza has assembled a world-class team of executives with deep domain and operational expertise to tackle identity-based threats that are increasingly prevalent across enterprises.
With the rise of Agentic technologies, enterprises need platforms like Veza more than ever. There’s a clear opportunity to define a new category—just as Okta did—and Veza is uniquely positioned to lead and win in this space.

Role & Responsibilities
Reporting directly to Co-Founder and CEO Tarun Thakur, the VP of Engineering will:

Scale the engineering organization from 80 to 300+ team members while ensuring innovation velocity and resource optimization—expanding existing teams and building new ones where needed.

Build and mentor a high-performing leadership bench, including engineering managers and technical directors, with a focus on long-term sustainability and growth.

Implement best-in-class engineering processes and workflows that support efficient development, on-time product delivery, high-quality deliverables, and effective management of customer escalations.

Deliver critical technical milestones that enable product expansion, meet evolving customer requirements, and support seamless scalability during hyper-growth.

Foster a strong, inclusive engineering culture rooted in innovation, collaboration, and accountability.

Define and track key performance metrics and create transparent career development paths to support retention and long-term team success.

About Veza

Veza is the identity security company. Identity and security teams use Veza to secure identity access across SaaS apps, on-prem apps, data systems, and cloud infrastructure. Veza solves the blind spots of traditional identity tools with its unique ability to ingest and organize permissions metadata in the Veza Authorization Graph. Global enterprises like Blackstone, Wynn Resorts, and Expedia trust Veza to visualize access permissions, monitor permissions activity, automate access reviews, and remediate privilege violations. Founded in 2020, Veza is headquartered in Redwood City, California, and is funded by Accel, Bain Capital, Ballistic Ventures, GV, Norwest Venture Partners, and True Ventures.

Job Description

About the job Vice President, Emerging Technologies

Location: United States (Remote and Hybrid)

Compensation: $285K – $350K/yr

A leading national healthcare organization is seeking a Vice President of Emerging Technologies to accelerate its mission through strategic, enterprise-wide innovation. This executive leader will champion the adoption of high-impact technologies, particularly AI, to simplify complex processes, enhance operational performance, and improve the experiences of donors, patients, and internal stakeholders.

The VP will operate as a strategic partner to senior leadership, identifying, evaluating, and advancing transformative technologies that drive measurable value. This includes shaping and executing the organization's emerging technology and AI strategy, enabling greater efficiency, improved outcomes, and long-term leadership in innovation within the healthcare ecosystem.

What You'll Do

Provide visionary, enterprise-level leadership to guide innovation strategy and align technology initiatives with organizational goals. Identify and implement innovation opportunities using emerging technologies, including AI, to enhance donor engagement, streamline operations, and support clinical and operational outcomes. Scout, assess, and recommend transformative technologies and trends that advance donor care, healthcare delivery, and operational efficiency. Develop and execute a comprehensive AI roadmap focused on high-impact, ROI-driven initiatives. Advise senior executives, embedding innovation into business strategy and ensuring alignment with mission and long-term vision. Champion the use of AI, automation, and digital tools to generate insights, improve donor and patient experience, and optimize supply chain performance. Drive cross-functional collaboration to ensure innovation initiatives are ethical, compliant, and integrated seamlessly into workflows. Build and maintain strategic partnerships and innovation ecosystems to accelerate transformation. Establish governance models that support responsible experimentation and scalable adoption. Lead and empower a multidisciplinary innovation team responsible for opportunity identification, piloting, and scaling solutions. Monitor regulatory changes and disruptive trends to maintain industry leadership.

Requirements

Education

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Data Science, Engineering, Healthcare Technology, or related field required. Master’s or higher in Business Administration, Artificial Intelligence, Data Analytics, or related discipline strongly preferred. Deep knowledge of AI, machine learning, cloud platforms, and digital transformation strategies. Familiarity with healthcare or life sciences regulatory environments (FDA, AABB, HIPAA, etc.).

Experience

8+ years of progressive leadership experience in emerging technologies, innovation strategy, or technology transformation. 6+ years leading large-scale AI or advanced analytics initiatives, preferably in healthcare, biotech, or donor-focused organizations. Demonstrated success implementing enterprise platforms or technology ecosystems. Proven track record of driving innovation and measurable business outcomes. Experience managing cross-functional teams, strategic partners, and vendors.

Skills

Strategic thinker with the ability to connect emerging technologies to operational and business goals. Exceptional leadership, communication, and collaboration skills. Strong technical acumen with the ability to convey complex concepts to executive and non-technical audiences. Excellent problem-solving and decision-making abilities. Ability to navigate regulated industries and complex stakeholder environments. Entrepreneurial, agile mindset with comfort in fast-evolving technology landscapes

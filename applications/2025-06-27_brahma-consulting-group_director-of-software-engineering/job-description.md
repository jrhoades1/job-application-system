Job Description
About the job

We are seeking a dynamic leader to transform and elevate our software development landscape. As a Software Engineering Manager, you will be entrusted with streamlining development lifecycles, implementing scalable improvement methodologies, and nurturing high-performing engineering teams that consistently deliver with speed and precision.

In this strategic role, you’ll be responsible for architecting change—focusing on operational maturity, team empowerment, and cutting-edge practices across Agile, Lean, and DevOps frameworks. Your mission is to eliminate inefficiencies, boost engineering output, and sustain a culture of adaptability and technical mastery.

Key Impact Areas:

Drive strategic modernization of software delivery processes to maximize team throughput and delivery quality.
Embed Agile mindsets and DevOps automation into core engineering behaviors, facilitating rapid, reliable, and repeatable software releases.
Mentor and enable developers and tech leads to internalize best practices and become catalysts for sustained performance improvement.
Analyze delivery trends and team metrics to expose friction points, evaluate experiment outcomes, and prioritize process interventions.
Promote cross-functional learning and transparency through collaborative sessions, shared rituals, and knowledge platforms.
Align all improvement efforts with compliance benchmarks and evolving product quality expectations.
Author clear documentation capturing methodologies, experiments, outcomes, and retrospectives for long-term traceability and replication.
Encourage continual professional development within engineering squads, reinforcing technical curiosity and long-term capability growth.

What You’ll Bring:

Strategic thinking paired with a sharp diagnostic lens to uncover hidden process debt.
Solid technical grasp of iterative development, deployment automation, and collaborative planning.
Adept at resolving barriers and unlocking potential through influence, empathy, and tactical execution.
Natural communicator capable of translating technical nuance into compelling narratives for stakeholders.
Proven ability to facilitate organizational change, even amid ambiguity or resistance.
Strong instincts for mentorship, team coaching, and growth enablement at scale.
Experience with performance monitoring, process instrumentation, and decision-making informed by quantitative insight.
Committed to personal and team development, with a forward-leaning mindset toward emerging trends and technologies.

Technology Proficiency:

Programming & UI : Proficient in C#, HTML, CSS, familiar with IDEs such as Visual Studio and JetBrains.
Automation & DevOps : Hands-on experience with Git, Bash scripting, CircleCI or similar CI/CD systems.
Testing : Competent in test automation using tools like xUnit, Postman, and load testing frameworks.
Project & Knowledge Management : Effective with Atlassian tools, specifically Jira and Confluence.

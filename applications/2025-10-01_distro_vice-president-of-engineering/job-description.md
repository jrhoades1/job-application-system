Job Description

About the job About Us We’re on a mission to help multi-unit restaurants deliver consistently amazing guest experiences. Every day, thousands of restaurants across North America rely on us to collect, analyze, and act on real-time feedback from their guests. With an Ovation survey taken every three seconds, we’re powering insights that have already saved restaurants millions in revenue — and we’re just getting started.

We’re a growth-stage company disrupting a legacy space. By combining innovation, speed, and customer obsession, we’re redefining how restaurants connect with their guests and how operators drive real impact.

Why Work For Us

At our core, we’re builders and problem-solvers who thrive on tackling big challenges. We don’t shy away from tough problems — we embrace them, using cutting-edge technology and creativity to develop solutions that transform an industry.

Joining us means becoming part of a fast-paced, ambitious, and collaborative team where your work matters every single day. You’ll help shape the future of guest experience in restaurants, push the boundaries of what’s possible, and make a tangible difference at scale.

If you’re passionate about innovation, energized by speed, and excited to disrupt a legacy industry with technology that impacts millions of people, you’ll feel right at home here.

The Role

We’re hiring a Vice President of Engineering to join our leadership team and report directly to the co-founder and CPO. This is a strategic role for a capable and deeply technical leader who brings both executive leadership and coding credibility. While your primary responsibility is leading the team and scaling our engineering capabilities, you must have a solid background in software development and be ready to contribute code when needed — especially in high-priority or time-sensitive situations.

We’re looking for someone who is passionate, driven, ambitious, and competitive - a leader who is relentless about winning and doesn’t accept losing as an option. You should thrive in fast-paced environments, bring energy and urgency to execution, and push your team to deliver at the highest level. The ideal candidate balances strategic innovation with operational excellence while driving the adoption of AI across our SaaS platform.

Key Responsibilities Define and execute the technical vision, infrastructure, and architecture to scale Ovation’s platform. Build, mentor, and grow a high-performing engineering team. Improve engineering processes, delivery velocity, and organizational effectiveness. Collaborate closely with product leadership to align technical execution with product strategy. Manage budget, technical spend, and resources to maximize velocity and efficiency. Lead the adoption and implementation of AI capabilities into Ovation’s products. Ensure uptime, reliability, and security of our SaaS platform. Establish strong hiring practices and performance review processes. Provide hands-on technical expertise when required — from reviewing architecture to occasionally writing production-quality code. Set standards for tooling, technical decision-making, and team accountability.

Requirements Proven experience as a VP of Engineering (or similar senior leadership role) in SaaS or cloud environments. Results-driven~ competitive, relentless about winning, and focused on measurable outcomes. Strong coding background and ability to contribute directly when needed. Demonstrated success scaling engineering teams in startup and growth-stage contexts. Prior experience implementing AI initiatives in enterprise-grade products. Technical depth in cloud infrastructure, enterprise software, and data analytics. Skilled at cross-functional collaboration across product, design, and leadership. Excellent executive communication and leadership skills. Experience managing budget, uptime, hiring, and operational KPIs.

Compensation Competitive base salary. Performance bonus tied primarily to revenue growth, with a secondary component for staying within GTM budget. Equity participation. Flexible PTO and Focus Fridays. 401(k) with company match. Health coverage support through monthly employer-funded QSEHRA reimbursements (choose the plan that works best for you).

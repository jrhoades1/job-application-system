Job Description
About the job

Vice President of Engineering

Remote (Global) | Full-time | Series C Developer Infrastructure Startup

Must be US Citizen or Green Card Holder

Must have VP or Director Eng experience within Series A-C startups and have worked within the dev tooling, dev infrastructure or developer platform space.

We’re helping a fast-growing and culture-focused, series-backed startup scale its engineering leadership by way of hiring a VP of Engineering. This globally distributed company offers a developer platform that makes it easy to deploy full-stack applications close to your users — in data centers around the world — without the burden of managing traditional infrastructure.

Think of it as a global app hosting service that automatically runs your application on servers nearest to your users, delivering faster performance and a better experience.

They’re now looking for a VP of Engineering to bring structure, leadership, and vision to a high-performing team of ~40 engineers.

🧭 About the Role

This is a defining leadership opportunity: you'll shape the future of engineering at a company doing truly novel work in developer infrastructure and distributed systems. You'll report to the CEO/Founder and collaborate closely with product and platform leaders to ensure delivery, scale, and team cohesion.

The ideal candidate is an exceptional people-first engineering leader with deep empathy, high standards, and the technical grounding to engage in architectural tradeoffs—without needing to be the most hands-on coder in the room.

🔧 What You'll Do

Lead, mentor, and grow a global engineering org of ~40 people, across multiple functions and disciplines.
Bring calm and clarity to a high-velocity, somewhat unstructured engineering environment.
Collaborate with product, ops, and platform teams to align engineering priorities with company goals.
Define and evolve how engineering is done—from hiring standards to processes to rituals.
Strike a balance between velocity and quality; innovation and operational excellence.
Support a high-performing team building with Go , Elixir , and Rust
Communicate clearly and frequently—especially in writing via Slack, Discord, and docs
Balance new feature development with technical debt, testing, and infrastructure improvements
Hire and develop A-level engineering talent across frontend, backend, and infrastructure roles
Ensure we build for both immediate impact and long-term extensibility

🧠 What We're Looking For

5+ years of engineering leadership at the VP or Director level in well-funded startups (Series A–D).
Strong familiarity with developer tools, dev platforms, dev infrastructure, or edge compute environments.
Experience managing 20+ engineers across distributed teams.
A background in systems, infrastructure, or platform engineering—enough to ask the right questions and weigh tradeoffs.
Excellent written communication skills and extreme attention to detail—most internal communication happens async and in writing.
Experience scaling teams, bringing order to ambiguity, and building cultures that blend speed with stability.
Looking for someone who can lead with empathy, bring structure, and win hearts and minds.

⚙️ Tech Snapshot (You don’t need to code daily, but you should understand the stack)

Languages: Go, Elixir, Rust
Infra: Lightweight VMs, distributed proxies, SQLite CRDTs, Anycast
Products: Developer platform, edge compute, container orchestration, PostgreSQL-as-a-service
Team: Fully remote, async-first, engineering-driven culture

Why Join

Help shape the engineering culture at a company redefining cloud infrastructure.
Work with brilliant engineers on low-level, performance-critical, globally distributed systems.
Join a company that values clarity, autonomy, and craft—not bureaucracy or buzzwords.
Have real impact—this isn’t just a job; it's a chance to leave a mark on a technical frontier.

Note : This role is fully remote, with employees across North America, Europe, and beyond. Occasional travel may be expected for team summits or off-sites.

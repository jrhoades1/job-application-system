About the job

About DrFirst

For 25 years, DrFirst has empowered providers and patients to achieve better health through intelligent medication management. We improve healthcare workflows and help patients start and stay on therapy with end-to-end solutions that enhance prescription access, affordability, and adherence. Our solutions help 100 million patients a year and are used by more than 420,000 prescribers, 71,000 pharmacies, 270 EHRs and health information systems, and over 2,000 hospitals in the U.S.

This is a great opportunity to be a part of a successful Healthcare IT company experiencing significant growth. Here you'll get to work with some of the smartest and most interesting people around; solving unique and complex challenges in healthcare on a scale matched by a few companies. If you get excited about stretching yourself in new ways, developing yourself to your fullest potential, care about working with smart colleagues; we want to talk to you!

Position Overview

Do you want to impact the health of 1/3 of the US population with a single deployment?
Are you passionate about developing services that pair real-time context with ML, LLM, NLP at scale?
Are you a visionary leader that wants to lead several teams to innovate clever solutions to critical healthcare issues and workflow?

If you answered yes to all these questions, we have the role for you!!

As VP of Application Development, you will be responsible for leading and mentoring multiple engineering teams in the design, development, and deployment of scalable AI-powered applications. You will play a critical role in shaping our technology strategy and driving innovation across the organization.

What You Will Work On

Lead and inspire: Provide technical leadership and mentorship to a talented team of engineers. Foster a culture of collaboration, innovation, and continuous improvement.
Architect and develop: Oversee the architecture and development of scalable and robust applications, leveraging both in-house AI models and third-party LLM and NLP technologies.
Drive AI strategy: Champion the use of AI across the organization. Identify opportunities to leverage AI to enhance existing products and create new ones.
Embrace modern technologies: Be a champion for modern software development practices, including event-driven architecture, cloud technologies (AWS, GCP), GitOps , and data lakes.
Ensure data integrity: Implement robust solutions for user/entity identity resolution and data classification.
Collaborate effectively: Work closely with cross-functional teams, including product management, data science, and operations, to ensure alignment and successful execution of projects.

Qualifications

10+ years of experience in software development, with at least 5 in a leadership role.
Proven track record of building and scaling high-performance applications in a fast-paced, agile environment.
Experience evolving technical workstreams/workflows and leading organizational transformations.
Deep understanding of AI/ML technologies, including LLMs, NLP, and event-driven architectures.
Hands-on experience with cloud platforms (AWS, GCP) and DevOps practices ( GitOps ).
Strong understanding of data lakes, data pipelines, and data security.
Experience with user/entity identity resolution and data privacy.
Ability to set a vision and inspire and motivate teams; can drive organizational change within and beyond functions.
Experienced delivering swiftly against deadlines across multiple projects in a fast-paced environment.
Stays up to date on knowledge and understanding of innovative technologies .
Experience in a highly regulated industry such as healthcare.
Excellent communication, interpersonal, and leadership skills.

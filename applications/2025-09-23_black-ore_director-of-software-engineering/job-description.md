About the job

About Us

Black Ore is building the leading AI platform for financial services. By combining LLMs, proprietary AI/ML and automation we accelerate core workflows for the industry, allow financial services professionals to be more productive and enable consumers to enhance their personal finance. Our flagship product, Tax Autopilot, combines AI with federal and state tax codes & regulations to simplify the tax preparation and review process for Certified Public Accountants (CPAs) and accounting firms.

Founded in 2022, we launched with $60 million in early stage funding from some of the world’s leading investors including a16z, Founders Fund, General Catalyst, Khosla Ventures, Oak HC/FT, Trust Ventures and leading tech founders/angel investors including Jason Gardner (Founder and CEO of Marqeta), Max Levchin (Founder of Paypal and Affirm), Tom Glocer (Former CEO of Thomson Reuters), Gokul Rajaram, and Mark Britto (EVP, CPO, PayPal).

Our team has an incredibly ambitious vision to completely transform the way businesses and consumers interact in financial services. We’re looking to hire strong team members to grow the team. Some of the traits we look for are:

Owner Mentality - Desire to take initiative, identify problems and implement solutions
Mission Driven - Passion for building solutions that reimagine how businesses and consumers operate
Intellectually Curious - Excitement going deep and building detailed understanding of the function, role, customer and problem space
Team Oriented - Ability to collaborate respectfully and put the team above the self

The Role

If you are looking for strictly a full time management/strategy role, this is not the opportunity for you. We are looking for innovative candidates primarily coming from a startup background.

We are seeking a highly experienced and motivated Director of Software Engineer to join our dynamic team. In this role, you will be responsible for building the future version of our products, driving innovation, and supporting a high-performing engineering culture. As a senior member of the Engineering team, you will work cross functionally and be expected to influence end to end the technology, product and user experience. You will be responsible for designing, implementing and deploying software components from the ground up through production. You will work both independently and with your team to deliver software successfully, constructively identify problems, and integrate solutions.

The Ideal candidate profile for this role is someone who is a builder at heart that still enjoys building products while also helping to establish a software development team. 90% of your time will be initially spent hands on building our platform, while also helping to grow and uplevel our engineering organization in the US.

Basic Qualifications:

Serve as a senior contributor and a go-to engineering expert
Design, develop, and deliver high-quality, scalable, and maintainable production code.
Drive engineering decisions and ensure adherence to best practices.
Guide a small team of engineers and set clear technical goals and objectives for the team, ensuring alignment with our engineering & product strategy
Facilitate technical discussions, resolve roadblocks, and promote a collaborative team environment.
Communicate complex technical concepts and project updates effectively to both technical and non-technical audiences
Collaborate closely with product managers, designers, and other stakeholders to translate business requirements into technical solutions.

What You'll Bring:

Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field (or equivalent practical experience).
Minimum 10+ years of experience in software engineering or machine learning
3+ years of experience in a Director, staff engineer, team lead, Sr. Staff or Principal level role
Expert level experience in any/all of the following : Python, JavaScript, TypeScript
Experience with frameworks, libraries and databases including but not limited to Node.JS, React, Angular, Vue, Express.js, NestJS, PostgreSQL, MongoDB, AWS, Azure, GCP, Docker, Kubernetes, etc.
Experience with hiring, scaling and leading a team
Proven track record of successfully shipping high-quality production code in a fast-paced environment.
Strong understanding of modern software development principles, architectural patterns, and best practices.
Demonstrated ability to effectively communicate, influence, and build relationships with executives and senior leadership.
Excellent written and verbal communication skills.

Bonus Points If You Have:

Working in a high growth startup
Experience with Machine Learning and AI architecture.
Experience in FinTech, particularly within the tax software industry

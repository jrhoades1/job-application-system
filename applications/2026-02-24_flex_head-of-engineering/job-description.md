## About Flex

Flex makes it easy for health and wellness brands to accept HSA and FSA payments online. Their technology verifies product eligibility in real time, handles split payments for eligible and non-eligible items, and can issue Letters of Medical Necessity during checkout. $150B in annual HSA/FSA spending potential. Early-stage startup: 11-50 employees, 41 on LinkedIn, 0.8 year median tenure. 583% company-wide growth over 2 years, 700% engineering growth.

Job Description

About the job About the Company

Joy 101 is a mission-driven startup founded by Hoda Kotb, dedicated to guiding everyone on their journey to joy. We offer a simple, affordable, science-backed approach to daily wellness through a beautifully designed mobile app. Inside the app, users discover motivational weekly plans, expert-led practices like breathwork and visualization, inspiring interviews, and more—all designed to help build joyful habits and a resilient mindset.

Backed by a seasoned team of operators and investors, Joy 101 is live and gaining momentum. Since our launch in late May 2025, we’ve seen exceptional early traction with over 80,000 email signups and almost 100,000 app downloads —and we’re just getting started. We’re now looking for a senior leader to help drive growth, deepen engagement, and evolve the product into a category-defining wellness platform.

About the Role

You’ll oversee the development and ongoing performance of our React Native mobile app, web frontend (React / Next.js), and backend api (Node / NestJS), ensuring seamless content delivery and an engaging user experience. You’ll work cross-functionally with product, design, and content teams to bring new features to life, manage infrastructure, and ensure platform stability and scalability. This role is ideal for a hands-on technical leader who is excited about wellness, community, and the power of great content to create meaningful change.

Key Responsibilities

Lead the architecture, development, and maintenance of our mobile app (React Native) and web platform (React/Next.js) and backend (Node / NestJS) . Oversee devops utilizing AWS services (Beanstalk, Batch, EC2, RDS, Cloudwatch, EventBridge, Terraform) Design and manage systems for our personalization algorithm and daily content publishing, including streaming audio/video and written content. Own and evolve the personalization algorithm over time. Assess AI tools and how these can be integrated to build a highly personalized experience. Concept and build new products that enhance the user experience, such as habit tracking, ticketing, community, and live event experiences. Build and optimize infrastructure using Firebase Authentication, Contentful, AWS, as well as managing third-party integrations. Identify and evolve the products while selecting and building the ideal technical stack. Collaborate with cross-functional teams to shape the product roadmap and user experience. Implement and uphold best practices in code quality, security, testing, and deployment workflows. Manage CI/CD pipelines, mobile app release cycles, and platform monitoring.

Qualifications

7+ years of hands-on engineering experience with deep expertise in React Native for mobile development. Strong front-end web development skills using React, Next.js, or similar frameworks. Proficiency in JavaScript/TypeScript, with a strong grasp of performance optimization, native integrations, and cross-platform challenges. Demonstrated experience with content delivery systems—especially for audio, video, and dynamic daily updates. Familiarity with Firebase, headless CMSs, and third-party analytics or payment tools. Ability to thrive in a lean, collaborative, early-stage environment. Mission-driven, with a strong interest in wellness, mental health, or self-development.

What You’ll Get

A senior leadership role with high ownership and impact. The chance to work on a mission-driven product backed by a celebrity founder with a global platform. A collaborative team culture focused on purpose, performance, and user well-being. Competitive compensation and meaningful equity, benefits, and 401k. Flexible, remote work environment.

Eligibility notice: Joy 101 is currently hiring only in the following states: California, Connecticut, Florida, Louisiana, New York, Pennsylvania, Texas, and Virginia. Only applicants who reside in one of these states will be considered for this role.

Joy 101 is an Equal Opportunity Employer and welcomes applicants from all backgrounds.

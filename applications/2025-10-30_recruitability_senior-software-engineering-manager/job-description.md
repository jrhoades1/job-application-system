Job Description
About the job

We’re hiring a Sr Software Engineering Manager — someone who can bring discipline, visibility, and leadership to how we deliver.

This is not your average manager role. This is a builder’s role. It’s your chance to work directly with visionary founders, shape the operating backbone of a high-growth company, and fast-track your career in ways most people only dream about.

Why This Role Is a Career Game-Changer

Direct Access to Leadership: Work side-by-side with our CEO and CTO, gaining firsthand mentorship and exposure to strategic decision-making.
High-Impact Environment: No “playbook-following” here. You’ll design and own the systems that drive delivery, client onboarding, and developer velocity.
Elite Team, High Standards: This is a championship team where performance matters. We pick hard things, we move fast, and we expect excellence — but the payoff is unmatched growth and opportunity.
Shape the Future of Commerce: The processes you create and the projects you lead will directly shape the infrastructure of e-commerce and logistics at scale.

Program Leadership & Execution

Own the planning, execution, and delivery of complex technical initiatives spanning OMS, WMS, integrations, and frontend.
Translate company priorities into structured roadmaps, clear milestones, and sprint-level execution plans.
Anticipate risks and dependencies, resolve blockers before they surface, and ensure programs stay on track.
Run and refine ceremonies (sprint planning, retrospectives, demos, reviews) that maximize focus, velocity, and transparency.
Build visibility across the org with dashboards, release schedules, and risk tracking that keep leadership confident and teams unblocked.

Implementation & Client Delivery

Lead technical onboarding and integration-heavy implementations in partnership with Customer Success.
Translate complex client requirements into actionable project plans for engineering.
Coordinate across third parties — warehouses, WMS providers, and marketplaces — to deliver seamless integrations.
Build direct trust with clients by combining technical fluency with business context.

Developer Velocity & Continuous Improvement

Partner closely with developers to remove blockers, prioritize high-leverage initiatives, and scale processes that allow engineers to ship faster.
Instill a QA-first mindset , ensuring every deliverable is tested against ripple effects across order flows, purchase orders, and financial data.

Qualifications Required

5+ years as a Engineering Manager, Program Manager, or Delivery Lead in SaaS, platform, or infrastructure companies.
Strong technical fluency: familiarity with the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js), serverless architecture, and AWS Lambda.
Experience working directly with engineering and product in Agile or hybrid environments.
Proficiency with project management tools (Jira, ClickUp, Asana, Notion, Linear).

Preferred

Startup or high-growth environment experience.
Background in e-commerce, supply chain, logistics tech, OMS, or WMS.
Familiarity with GitHub workflows, CI/CD systems, or modern release management practices.

What Success Looks Like

Roadmaps and sprint deliverables are clear, visible, and aligned with company priorities.
Engineers are shipping faster with fewer interruptions.
Complex client implementations go live smoothly and add measurable business value.
Leadership has full confidence in program visibility, predictability, and quality.

What’s In It For You

Competitive Salary + Equity — aligned with the value you create.
Direct Mentorship from Founders — condensing decades of experience into years of growth for you.
Unlimited PTO & Remote Flexibility — we trust you to perform, not clock hours.
Health & 401(k) Benefits — competitive benefits for you and your family.
Elite Career Acceleration — five years here will prepare you to lead at the highest levels anywhere.

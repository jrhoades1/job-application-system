Job Description

About the job Senior Engineering Manager – Search & Machine Learning

About The Role

We are seeking a Senior Engineering Manager to lead our Search and Machine Learning team, driving innovation in how users discover and access critical services. In this role, you will guide a team of engineers and data scientists to deliver scalable, intelligent solutions that improve relevance, personalization, and efficiency in a high-impact domain. Your leadership will shape both the technical strategy and the culture of the team, ensuring a balance between rapid delivery, long-term investment, and engineering excellence.

Why This Role Matters

Search and discovery are at the heart of any digital marketplace. By advancing our ML-driven search systems, you will help millions of people find what they need faster and more effectively. Beyond technology, you’ll influence product direction, mentor teams, and build a collaborative environment where engineers can thrive. This is an opportunity to combine technical depth with strategic vision and deliver solutions that make a tangible impact on users’ lives.

What You’ll Do

Lead and mentor a multidisciplinary team of Machine Learning Engineers and Data Scientists, fostering growth, accountability, and innovation. Define technical strategy and partner closely with product leaders to translate business goals into scalable engineering solutions. Oversee the delivery of high-performance search and recommendation features that improve relevance, speed, and reliability for end users. Guide architectural decisions with an emphasis on security, scalability, uptime, and future readiness. Drive adoption of best practices in ML, software development, and DevOps, ensuring systems evolve with changing business and user needs. Influence engineering culture by shaping career development frameworks, mentoring programs, and cross-team initiatives that promote inclusivity and collaboration. Collaborate with stakeholders across engineering, product, and design to balance experimentation with execution, and ensure alignment on priorities. Leverage GenAI and emerging technologies to push the boundaries of what is possible in real-time search and discovery experiences.

What You Bring

Proven experience leading engineering teams focused on consumer-facing products, with a strong emphasis on search, data, and ML-driven applications. Hands-on background in delivering large-scale software systems or data infrastructure, either as an individual contributor or manager. Deep understanding of software development methodologies, including those tailored for Machine Learning and Data Science initiatives. Track record of shipping impactful features to external customers in high-scale environments. Experience with cloud-native architectures and microservices, particularly in AWS or other large-scale environments. Strong communication skills, with the ability to build consensus across technical and non-technical stakeholders. Entrepreneurial mindset, bias toward action, and ability to navigate ambiguity. Commitment to fostering diverse and inclusive teams, with a leadership style rooted in empathy and respect.

Benefits & Perks

Flexible hybrid work model with a centrally located office hub. Unlimited paid time off with a focus on work-life balance. Comprehensive health benefits, including fully covered options for medical, dental, and vision. 401(k) with company match. Wellness programs, including fitness and mindfulness partnerships. Sabbatical opportunities for long-tenured employees. Paid parental leave and fertility/family planning support. Catered meals, stocked kitchens, and commuter benefits. Employee-led resource groups and clubs to foster community and belonging.

Compensation

We are committed to equitable compensation practices. The base salary range for this position is $230,000 – $345,000 USD, depending on experience, skills, and qualifications. Additional variable pay and equity opportunities may be included as part of the total compensation package.

This is a chance to lead a team at the intersection of machine learning and large-scale consumer platforms—delivering innovation that directly improves user experiences while shaping the future of search and discovery technology.
